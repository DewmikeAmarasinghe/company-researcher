# Executive Summary: Ceylon Cold Stores PLC (CCS) (elephant house)

## 1. Founders and Leadership
- **Founder:** *Arthur Von Possner* (German engineer) founded the original Colombo Ice Company in 1866.
- **Brand origin:** 1883 Elephant trademark introduced on aerated water; brand evolved to Elephant House.
- **Current leadership (early 2026):**  
  - **Krishan Balendra** — Chairperson (Board)  
  - **Daminda Gamlath** — President, Consumer Foods & Executive Director  
  - **Nelindra Fernando** — CFO, Consumer Foods  
  - **Gihan Cooray** — Executive Director  
  - Independent Non-Executive Directors: **Renuka Fernando**, **Shivaan Kanag-Isvaran**, **Harin De Silva Wijeyeratne**  
- **Parent company:** subsidiary of **John Keells Holdings (JKH)**

## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- **Core focus:** manufacturing, marketing, and distribution of consumer foods under the Elephant House brand (beverages and frozen confectionery), with integrated retail via Keells supermarkets.
- **Revenue streams:**
  - **Manufacturing:** Elephant House beverages (CSDs, energy drinks, water) and frozen confectionery (ice creams, popsicles).
  - **Retail:** Keells supermarkets via Jaykay Marketing Services (JMSL) within the JKH Group.
  - **International royalties & sales:** royalties from RCPL (India) and concentrate sales; export services to 25+ countries.
- **Pricing strategy:** competitive pricing to defend leading market position; emphasis on high-margin impulse and premium lines (e.g., IMORICH ice creams) and value-added offerings.

## 3. Products and Services
- **1) Beverages Portfolio**
  - Carbonated Soft Drinks (CSD):  
    - Elephant House Ginger Beer (EGB), Cream Soda, KIK Cola, fruit-flavored sodas (Orange Crush, Necto, Orange Barley, Lemonade, Portello), mixers (Soda Water, Tonic Water)  
  - Fruit-based beverages: under the **Fit-O** brand (natural fruit juices)  
  - Dairy: fresh and flavored milks (Chocolate, Vanilla, Strawberry); unsweetened variants  
  - Energy drinks; Hydration: still and sparkling water
- **2) Frozen Confectionery**
  - Premium range: **IMORICH** (Hazelnut Chocolate Gelato, Blueberry Cheesecake, Raspberry Cheesecake)  
  - Everyday/bulk tubs: Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, Fruit & Nut, Karutha Kolumban (Mango), 500ml–4L sizes  
  - Impulse products: Cones (Wonder), Popsicles, Sticks & Bars (e.g., Wonder Bar, Major Max)  
  - Ice cubes: sold under **Icies** sub-brand
- **3) Processed Meats**
  - Sausages; Cold cuts (frozen meat products)
- **4) Retail and Services**
  - Supermarkets: Keells chain (via JMSL)
  - Export services: beverages and ice cream to 25+ countries
  - Manufacturing services: PET bottling and can manufacturing for CCS and third parties

## 4. Company History
- **Founding & early growth:**
  - 1866: The Ice Kompaniya founded as Colombo Ice Company by Arthur Von Possner
  - 1883: Elephant trademark introduced on aerated water
  - 1894: Tom Walker acquires and merges to form New Colombo Ice Company Limited
- **Milestones:**
  - 1921–1934: Modernization (bottling, storage) and diversification (ice cream, milk)  
  - 1932–1935: Acquired Ceylon Creameries; began frozen foods imports
  - 1941: Renamed to Ceylon Cold Stores Limited
  - 1964: First Ceylonese Chairman (Mallory Wijesinghe)
  - 1970: Listed on Colombo Stock Exchange
  - 1980: Initiated international exports (Maldives)
  - 1991: Acquired by John Keells Holdings (JKH)
  - 1998: Major soft drink bottling plant in Ranala, Kaduwela
  - 2010: Relaunched brand and introduced **KIK Cola**
  - 2016: 150th anniversary
  - 2018: The Colombo Ice Company ice cream plant in Seethawaka
  - 2024–2025: Expanded internationally (RCPL India, Australia entry)

## 5. Competitive Landscape
- **Beverages (CSDs & juices):**  
  - Primary local leader: CCS Elephant House (~43%–47% market share)  
  - Major rivals: **Coca-Cola Beverages Sri Lanka** (~40%), **PepsiCo/Varun Beverages** (~10%), Smack Innovations, Ole (Cargills Ceylon), Kik Cola (local)
- **Frozen Confectionery (ice cream):** local and regional rivals include Cargills Quality Foods (Magic), Highland Milco, Kotmale (Cargills), Pelwatte Dairy, Alerics Ice Cream
- **Processed Meats:** Keells Food Products (KFP) and Cargills Quality Foods; Pussalla Meat Producers
- **Retail:** Keells Supermarkets vs Cargills Food City, Arpico Supercentre, Sathosa
- CCS is the market leader in Sri Lanka’s CSD and a dominant ice cream player with strong retail integration via Keells

## 6. Market Position
- **Market share (Beverages/CSD):** ~43%–47% in Sri Lanka
- **Competitor shares:** Coca-Cola ~40%, PepsiCo/Varun Beverages ~10%
- **Brand ranking:** *1st* in Brand Finance Sri Lanka 100 Most Valuable Brands 2025 (Food & Beverage); *Brand of the Year* for Elephant House Ice Cream (SLIM Brand Excellence Awards 2025)
- **Industry stature:** Market leader in CSD and Frozen Confectionery; diversified through Keells retail and export operations
- **Financial scale (2024/25):** Revenue ~LKR 157.9 billion; PAT ~LKR 5.96 billion

- **Opportunities:**

  - Digital transformation to improve distribution, targeted marketing, and resilience against depreciation
