# Executive Summary: DirectPay

## 1. Founders and Leadership
- **Founding year and founders:** Founded in **2017** by a team of technology entrepreneurs led by **Kanishka Weeramunda**; co-founders include **Dinesh Karunathilaka** and **Sasindu Pathiranage**; **Avindi Perera** identified as an original initiator.
- **Current CEO & founders’ leadership (2026):** 
  - **Kanishka Weeramunda** — **CEO** & Founder
  - **Disney Fernando** — Managing Director
  - **Sasindu Pathiranage** — **Vice President - Digital Payment Platforms** (formerly COO/CSO)
  - **Maduranga Ginigaddara** — **CFO**
  - **Rohan Muttiah** — **Non-Executive Director**
  - **Mariya Kaed** — **CMO**
- **Founders’ background:** Kanishka has over 20 years in IT and 8 years in fintech; leadership emphasizes rapid growth and strategic partnerships.

## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- **Business model:** Fintech startup acting as a cashless payments facilitator and payment aggregator; bridges merchants, banks, and customers with a modular, platform-first architecture; offers **Platform as a Service (PaaS)** to banks and fintechs.
- **Revenue streams:**
  - **Transaction commissions** on processed payments
  - **Monthly service fees** (subscription plans)
  - **Device leasing/sales** of Android Smart POS devices
  - **Tokenization fees** (e.g., **LKR 8** per card per month)
  - **Custom software solutions** for banks/financial institutions
- **Pricing strategy:** Tiered plans designed to scale with merchant size:
  - Internet Payment Gateway (IPG): **Starter 3.5%** + Free or higher plans; monthly fees scale from Free to **LKR 8,000**
  - Virtual POS (VPOS): **Starter 3.5%** + Free or higher monthly fees; limits increase per plan
  - Plans include **Starter**, **Pro**, and **Privilege** with respective transaction caps and monthly charges
- **Key product types:** Internet Payment Gateway, VPOS, physical POS, payment links, recurring payments, and related merchant services.

## 3. Products and Services
- **Core products/services:**
  - **Mobile payment apps** for consumers
  - **Internet Payment Gateway (IPG)**
  - **Payment links** for remote payments
  - **Subscription/recurring payment management**
  - **Bill settlement services**
  - **Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS)** for banks and fintechs
  - **Android Smart POS devices** (leasing/sales)
  - **Physical POS (Sunmi P2 Pro/Lite)** devices
  - **Merchant App**, **Consumer App**, and **Web Portal**
  - **QR-based payments** via LANKAQR and JustPay networks
  - **Tokenization & secure card storage** (no card data stored on DirectPay systems)
- **Key integrations:** LANKAQR, LankaPay CEFTS/JustPay, Visa, Mastercard; partnerships with major banks for IPG and acquiring services.

## 4. Company History
- **Founding story and timeline:**
  - 2017: DirectPay runner-up at Hemas Slingshot Startup Battle (Disrupt Asia 2017)
  - February 2018: Official launch as a fintech for digital payments
  - 2018: Won APICTA Gold (Sri Lanka) and NBQSA Best Startup Award
  - 2020: Processed LKR 2 billion in 20 months
  - June 2021: Transaction value surpassed LKR 3 billion
  - August 2021: Partnered with Commercial Bank of Ceylon for customized digital solutions
  - February 2023: 5-year anniversary; reached LKR 13.5 billion in transactions
  - October 2024: Reported 1000x growth in 60 months
  - Present: GovPay integration with government ecosystem; GovPay surpasses LKR 1 billion in digital transactions and connects 200+ government institutions
- **Notable milestones:** PCI DSS compliance milestone (2025); CBC branding and partnerships with major banks; recognized with multiple regional ICT awards.

## 24. Pricing Information
- **IPG pricing (merchants):**
  - Plan 1 (Starter): Commission 3.5%, Monthly fee: Free, Per-transaction limit: LKR 50,000
  - Plan 2 (Pro): Commission 2.9%, Monthly fee: **LKR 3,000**, Per-transaction limit: LKR 250,000
  - Plan 3 (Privilege): Commission 2.6%, Monthly fee: **LKR 8,000**, Per-transaction limit: LKR 1,000,000
  - Tokenization fee: **LKR 8** per card per month (Plan 2/3)
- **VPOS pricing:**
  - Plan 1 (Starter): Commission 3.5%, Monthly fee: Free, Max transactions: 50
  - Plan 2 (Pro): Commission 2.9%, Monthly fee: **LKR 500**, Max transactions: 100
  - Plan 3 (Privilege): Commission 2.6%, Monthly fee: **LKR 750**, Max transactions: Unlimited
  - Per-transaction limits: up to LKR 25,000 (Plan 1), 50,000 (Plan 2), 100,000 (Plan 3)
- **Physical POS pricing:**
  - Plan 1: Commission 3.5%, Monthly device charge: **LKR 3,500** (36-month contract)
  - Plan 2: Commission 2.9%, Monthly device charge: **LKR 4,000** (36-month)
  - Plan 3: Commission 2.6%, Monthly device charge: **LKR 4,750** (36-month)
  - Device-free commitment requires minimum monthly processing: Plan 1: LKR 500,000; Plan 2: LKR 650,000; Plan 3: LKR 800,000
- **Customer fees:** No setup fees for merchants; customer charges capped at up to **LKR 50** for Direct Debit via LankaPay
