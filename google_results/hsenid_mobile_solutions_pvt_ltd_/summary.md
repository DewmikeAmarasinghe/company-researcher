# Executive Summary: hSenid Mobile Solutions (Pvt) Ltd 

## 1. Founders and Leadership
- **Founder & CEO (2026):** **Dinesh Saparamadu** – Founder of the hSenid Group; CEO of **hSenid Mobile Solutions** and **Chairman** of the hSenid Group. (Note: some sources previously listed another CEO for 2024–2025; current 2026 records identify Dinesh in this role.)

## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- **Business model:** B2B focus serving telecommunications, banking, and large enterprises; ecosystem approach to connect telcos, businesses, and consumers (B2B2C).
- **Product as a Service:** Platform as a Service (PaaS) and Software as a Service (SaaS) with CPaaS, API gateways, and telco-grade platforms.
- **Revenue streams:**
  - Recurring subscription revenue for cloud-based solutions.
  - Product sales & software licensing (e.g., Telco Application Platform, Open API Gateway, USSD Gateway, banking platforms).
  - Professional services, systems integration, and consulting.
  - Outsourcing (onsite/offshore resource delivery).
  - Other ventures via the broader hSenid group (e.g., hospitality services like Mount Havana).
- **Pricing strategy:** Value-based pricing; flexible pay-as-you-go and session/request-based charging for CPaaS and API platforms; customization and integration often lead to project-based or tailored pricing; emphasis on reducing TCO via in-house capabilities (e.g., SIGTRAN stack).

## 4. Company History
- **1997:** Founded by **Dinesh Saparamadu** under hSenid Software International with 3 employees in a single room; initial focus on HR software before expanding to telecom/mobile sectors.
- **1999:** Secured first major clients: Sri Lankan Airlines and Sri Lanka Telecom.
- **2005:** Incorporation of **hSenid Business Solutions (hBS)** as a dedicated HRIS provider.
- **2008:** Expansion into Africa.
- **2012:** 15th anniversary; recognized as a pioneer in HR and Mobile IT solutions.
- **2016:** Flagship brand rebranded to **PeoplesHR**.
- **2021:** hSenid Business Solutions PLC listed on the Colombo Stock Exchange (first Sri Lankan enterprise software company to go public).
- **2024:** Gold Award at the 32nd Annual Export Awards; named “Technology Resilient Company” at Digital Trust Awards.
- **2025–26:** Expansion into **AI and Data Solutions**, GenAI copilots, predictive intelligence, and 5G-ready SMSC tech; focus on AI-driven data services and cloud/SaaS transformation.
