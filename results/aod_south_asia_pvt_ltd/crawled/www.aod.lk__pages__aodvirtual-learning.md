#  E-Learning 
AOD’s degree programmes are now taught online for your convenience and flexibility. These courses will be led by an international faculty and provide the students will endless resources for them to complete their undergraduate degree. 
  

AOD is offering three categories of online learning: online, offline and blended learning model. We aim to provide the best form of education for all our students and we believe that these options are aimed to succeed. 
**How are we offering online learning resources?**
Students will experience online learning in the comfort of their homes. Students will participate in online class sessions and submit assignments via the learning portal. In addition, they will be provided with feedback for tutorials, presentations and project crits. They will be provided with the immersive resources that AOD has to offer.
