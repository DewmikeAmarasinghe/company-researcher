#  AOD - De Montfort University, UK 
**De Montfort University (DMU)** has an international reputation as one of leading British University for art and design in the UK .It is the result of a distinctive combination of outstanding academic research and teaching, innovative programmes, traditional values and social and cultural diversity. Since its foundation DMU has developed into a successful regional, national and international institution. Students from DMU are working at companies and institutes such as BMW, Calvin Klein, Cummins, HSBC, the House of Commons, Network Rail and Riot Games.
The DMU Partnership enables AOD to provide Post graduates programmes in design for the very first time for students based in Sri Lanka.
**Why get your De Montfort MA at AOD?**  

Offering courses designed to increase your employability. The offered design postgraduate course allows you to go further in your knowledge and your career. You can become a specialist, explore the frontier of your subject area with leading researchers, gain accreditation or change trajectory.
