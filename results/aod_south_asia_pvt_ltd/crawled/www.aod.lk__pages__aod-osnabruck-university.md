#  AOD - Osnabrück University 
**Osnabrück University** founded in about 50 years ago, is a young, vibrant university in northwest Germany that is renowned for its research and teaching in the areas of Humanities, Social Sciences, Science, Law and Business Administration/Economics.
This partnership with a university in Germany has facilitated collaboration on academic projects in the field of fashion and textile design, Exchange of ideas to develop an empirical research based project reviewing Sri Lanka’s textile handloom industry. Our academic exchange program with Osnabrück University is focused on fostering better understanding of design in combination of tradition and modernity.
