## 1. Founders and Leadership
- Linda Speldewinde — Founder and Chairperson of **AOD** ; drives bridging local heritage with international design education; founder of **Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Sri Lanka**, **Sri Lanka Design Week**, and **Urban Island** ; promotes sustainable craft ecosystems including Garments Without Guilt initiatives
- Lin Gong-Deutschmann — Managing Director, **AOD & Design corp group** ; oversees operations ; emphasizes industry–craft collaboration and youth empowerment
- Prof. Robert Meeder — CEO since **September 2019** ; leads efforts to establish a South Asian knowledge hub in design and innovation ; background in luxury fashion sustainability and design education
- Partnerships and programs — **Northumbria University** partnership enables UK-degree pathways in Colombo ; **De Montfort University (DMU)** postgraduate programs ; **AOD Graduate School** offering MA programs
- Campus hub — **Colombo Innovation Tower** hosts AOD’s campus and serves as catalyst space for collaboration among businesses researchers and students
- Initiatives and impact — led **Sri Lanka Design Festival**, **Island Craft Project**, **Urban Island** and **FashionMarket.lk** ; efforts advance Sri Lanka’s design profile and integrate local crafts with international education
- Governance and strategy — leadership team focuses on industry linkages and scalable design education across South Asia
- Global recognition — leadership has positioned AOD as a conduit for design education internationalization
- Alumni outcomes — governance choices support high employability and global exposure for graduates

## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- UK-degree partnership model — delivers Northumbria University UK degrees in Sri Lanka through AOD; students enrolled as Northumbria University students
- Degree certainty — identical UK certificates; graduates can complete in Colombo or transfer to the UK; 100% international faculty; Northumbria examination board moderation
- Industry integration — strong links with built-in internships; graduate employment program reported as 100% success for the last 7 years
- Programs and delivery — full-time BA (Hons) across multiple design disciplines; foundations diplomas; masters available; e-learning options with three modes: online offline blended
- Revenue streams — tuition/degree fees aligned to Northumbria-based programs; application fees for local and international students; industry internships add contextual value
- Financing options — flexible study financing including Study Now Pay Later; 0% interest 12-month plans for selected banks; up to 20% savings on full payment options
- Recognition and governance — Sri Lanka University Grants Commission (UGC) recognition; access to Northumbria resources; active alumni network
- Learning ecosystem — MOODLE-based VLE with university-backed resources; online learning supported by international faculty
- Market positioning — revenue model blends domestic delivery with international degree legitimacy to attract local and regional cohorts
