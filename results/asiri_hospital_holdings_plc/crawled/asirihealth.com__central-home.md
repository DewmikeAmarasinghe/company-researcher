##### Diabetes Care
At Asiri Central Diabetes Care, we prioritize your health and well-being. We understand the challenges and complexities that come with managing diabetes, which is why we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalized care to meet your unique needs. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to supporting you every step of the way on your journey towards better health. With our tailored approach and state-of-the-art facilities, we can help you decrease the burden of diabetic care while empowering you to take control of your self-management. At Asiri Central, we pride ourselves on being among the best for diabetes care, where your health and satisfaction are our top priorities.
### Our Services
Diabetes Diagnostics
  

  * Laboratory investigations to screen for diabetes
  * Diabetes diagnosis and regular follow-up tests
  * Full screening for kidney disease


Some of our Diabetes Screening Packages include a consultation with our Clinical Psychologist for counselling if required.
Diabetes Foot Care Clinic (Podiatry Clinic)
  

  * Total foot assessment for neuropathy and peripheral vascular disease: A comprehensive evaluation to detect nerve damage and circulation issues in the feet.
  * Nail clipping/Ingrown nail clipping/Corn removal: Professional services for maintaining healthy nails and addressing common foot issues like ingrown nails and corns.
  * Callosity removal: Expert removal of calluses to prevent discomfort and reduce the risk of complications for individuals with diabetes.
  * Special and customized footwear for diabetic patients: Tailored footwear solutions designed to provide optimal support and reduce the risk of foot problems in individuals living with diabetes.


For tailored advise on nutrition for Diabetics, our Dieticians/Nutrition Physician provides, 
  

  * Personalized meal plans tailored to diabetes management and to help control blood sugar levels effectively.
  * Education on healthy living with diabetes empowering individuals to make informed lifestyle choices.
  * Understanding diabetes, foot care, proper footwear, and insulin pen usage are essential for optimal diabetes management.


We‚Äôre committed to helping prevent full onset of diabetes and to assisting those affected by it manage the condition to live a good quality life.

Are you concerned about your sleep? 
Are you sleeping enough? 
Is your sleep structure good? 
Do you have any sleep related disorder that is hindering you from achieving your best? 
If you have loud snoring, fall asleep during the day or feel tired all the time and feel sleepy despite having had around 8 hours of sleep the previous night; you may be suffering from a sleep disorder! 
During normal sleep, muscles that control the tongue and soft palate keep the airway open. If these muscles relax, the airway contracts and this may cause snoring and breathing difficulties. If your breathing is obstructed during sleep completely or partially, for five or more instances in an hour, it indicates that you have Obstructive Sleep Apnoea (OSA) which is quite a significant and common sleep disorder. Such disorders often go undiagnosed but the consequences of not receiving early treatment can be serious. 
_It is linked to problems such as,_
  * High blood pressure in the lungs (pulmonary hypertension)
  * High blood pressure
  * Depression
  * Stroke
  * Heart failure
  * Coronary artery disease
  * Hormone imbalance
  * Irregular heart rhythms


_Symptoms of OSA_
  * Excessive snoring 
  * Pauses in breathing during sleep 
  * Excessive daytime sleepiness or fatigue
  * Falling asleep easily during the day 
  * Fragmented sleep
  * Morning headaches 
  * Excessive movement while sleeping


_What should you do if you have these symptoms?_
  * **Screening:** Answer a simple set of questions (questionnaire linked below) and give it to your doctor, who will assess your level of risk. This questionnaire is also available at the Reception Desk of any Asiri Group Hospital. Pls advise on how best to include the questionnaire, which I will be sharing along with this document.
  * **Diagnosis:** Depending on your level of risk, your doctor may recommend a sleep test which can be performed at Asiri Central Hospital. We have the latest sleep lab with facilities for Level I and II polysomnogram.
  * **Treatment:** After assessing your sleep test results your doctor will explain your sleep structure to you - how much time you spend in deep sleep, what problems are detected in each stage of sleep, whether you have sleep apnoea, type and severity, any other sleep disorder. Depending on the diagnoses, he may then recommend treatment which usually involves using a facemask and a CPAP therapy device (CPAP stands for Continuous Positive Airway Pressure and is a treatment that uses mild air pressure to keep the airways open) during the night.
