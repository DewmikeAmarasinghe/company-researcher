## History
Established in 1980, Asiri Hospitals PLC, operating under the brand Asiri Health, is Sri Lanka's premier private healthcare provider. Counting almost 4 decades of excellence in healthcare, Asiri Health is owned by the Softlogic Group.
Asiri Laboratories: Originating at Asiri Medical Hospital, Asiri Laboratories has expanded to over a 100 locations across Sri Lanka, conducting an average of 14,500 tests daily.
  

  

## Milestones
  

## 2000
Asiri Surgical services were inaugurated, introducing specialized surgical care to meet the evolving healthcare needs. 
## 2007
A significant expansion took place with the acquisition of ASHA Central and the subsequent opening of Asiri Hospital Matara, contributing to our growing network of healthcare facilities. 
## 2010
Asiri Central Hospital was established, and the acquisition of Matara Medi House further solidified our commitment to providing comprehensive healthcare solutions. 
## 2018
The strategic expansion included the acquisition of Hemas Hospital Galle and the associated chain of laboratories, along with the inauguration of Asiri AOI Cancer Centre (Pvt) Ltd, marking a proactive approach towards addressing diverse healthcare requirements. 
## 2019
The establishment of Asiri Hospital Kandy, giving easier access to advanced healthcare services to the Central and Northern Provinces. 
## 2021
The launch of the Asiri Nova IVF and Fertility Centre, catering to an increasing demand for subfertility treatment in the country. 
## 2023
The launch of Asiri Academy of Health Sciences represented a pivotal moment in advancing medical education and training, further enriching the healthcare landscape with knowledgeable and skilled professionals 
## 1980
Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC commenced its operations as a limited liability company, marking the inception of a journey committed to providing quality healthcare. 
## 1984
The expansion of services was initiated, encompassing the addition of Outpatient (OPD) and Diagnostic services to further enhance the comprehensive healthcare offerings. 
## 1986
A significant milestone was achieved with the establishment of the Nurses Training School (NTS), underscoring our dedication to the development and education of healthcare professionals. 
## 1987
Introduction of Inpatient treatment was implemented, broadening the spectrum of medical care provided by Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC. 
## 1991
A momentous achievement was realized with the listing of the company on the Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE), reflecting its growth and prominence in the healthcare sector. 
## 1995
Diagnostic services witnessed an expansion to Kandy, extending our reach and accessibility for communities in different regions.
