## About Organization
Asiri Group of Hospitals is the largest hospital chain in Sri Lanka’s private sector healthcare industry with 6 hospitals and a laboratory chain island wide. Asiri Central Hospital is a flagship entity of the Group with global accreditation standards of Joint Commission International (JCI).  
With its luxurious advanced medical facilities, this 14 storied multi-specialty general hospital is a one-stop medical centre that offers diagnostic, therapeutic and intensive care facilities. Focusing on certain critical disciplines such as Cardiac Centre, Bone Marrow Transplant Unit, Brain & Spine Centre, Stroke Unit, Oncology Unit, Cosmetic Centre, Urology Unit, Interventional Radiology, and Kidney Transplant Centre. These Centers of Excellence bring in new hope to thousands of patients each year. They are supported by a team of highly-trained and experienced in-house Consultants, Surgeons, medical officers and nursing staff.
