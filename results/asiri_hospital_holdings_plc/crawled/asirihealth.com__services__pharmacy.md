## Services - Healthcare Services
# Pharmacy
Home / Services - Healthcare Services / **Pharmacy**
  
  

The medication management processes and its practices in all Asiri Pharmacies are guided by the world’s best standards set by both JCI and ACHSI accreditation bodies.
Located close to consultation rooms in all our hospitals, Asiri Pharmacies are easily accessible to patients for quick, easy pick up of prescribed medication.
## Our Products and Services
  * 24/7 OPD, delivery and pick-up services
  * Over-the-counter (OTC) products, prescription-only medicine (POM), and surgical products at Government-approved prices
  * GPP standards implemented and complied
  * All Government regulations are followed at all times
  * 3-dot method used to ensure safety (three people check the quality of the medication and the issue vs. prescription)
  * Strict cold chain maintenance for vaccines and heat-sensitive products
  * Only Government-approved registered drugs sold
  * Appropriate patient counseling is provided whenever applicable
  * Extemporaneous preparations
  * Baby and mother care products
  * Portable diagnostic devices such as Glucometers, Blood pressure meters, etc.
  * Surgical devices
  * Orthopedic support and mobilizing accessories
  * Nutraceutical products


## Highlights at a Glance
  * Quality is assured
  * Prescriptions are required for the purchase of any prescription-only medicine
  * Drugs selected through Drug Therapeutic Committee
  * SLMC Registered pharmacist on duty at any given time
