# Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC, announced as Platinum Sponsor for Medicare 2025 – International Healthcare Exhibition
_Businesscafe – Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC, Sri Lanka’s leading private healthcare provider, has been proudly announced as the Platinum Sponsor for Medicare 2025 – International Healthcare Exhibition._
This partnership reaffirms Asiri’s commitment to advancing the nation’s healthcare sector and fostering medical excellence.  

The 14th edition of Medicare – International Healthcare Exhibition 2025 is scheduled to take place from 28th February to 2nd March 2025 at the BMICH, Colombo.
  
This prestigious event will bring together industry leaders, healthcare professionals, and institutions to showcase advancements in medical technology, healthcare services, and wellness solutions.  

Asiri Hospitals has consistently been at the forefront of medical innovation and excellence in Sri Lanka, setting benchmarks in patient care, cutting-edge medical technology, and specialized treatments.
  
At Medicare 2025, Asiri will showcase its Centers of Excellence in Neurosurgery, Cardiac Surgery, and Oncology, along with its renowned IVF Programme.
  
Additionally, the hospital group will highlight key programs and initiatives, including the newly established Asiri Academy of Health Sciences, which focuses on training the next generation of healthcare professionals.  

At Medicare 2025, Asiri Health will showcase its Centres of Excellence for Brain & Spine, Heart and Cancer Care.
  
Also, it’s Bone Marrow Transplant programme and very successful Fertility Centre, along with representation for Asiri Laboratories.
  
Additionally, Asiri Health will highlight key new programs and initiatives, including the newly established Asiri Academy of Health Sciences, which focuses on training the next generation of healthcare professionals.  

With Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC’s steadfast dedication to healthcare excellence, its role as Platinum Sponsor further strengthens Medicare 2025’s vision of shaping a healthier future for Sri Lanka.
