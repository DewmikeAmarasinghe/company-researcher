## Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC Company Profile
Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC operates hospitals and provides healthcare service in Sri Lanka. It operates through Pre Care, Post Care, Pharmaceutical, and Other segments. The company operates centers, including heart, brain and spine, blood cancer and bone marrow transplant, stroke, interventional radiology, and kidney transplant unit centers, as well as urology, mother and baby care, cancer, and IVF and fertility centers; and mindfulness, vaccination, wellness, and community centers. It also provides 24-hour healthcare services, such as accident and emergency, ambulance, electrocardiogram, laboratory, CT, ultrasound scanning, x-ray, outpatient care, pharmacy, and other healthcare services. In addition, the company offers health and wellness services comprising home health, antenatal classes, diabetes care, mobile laboratory and ECG, vaccination center, and sleep study and sleep apnea tests. Further, it holds investments in the healthcare industry. The company was incorporated in 1980 and is based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC operates as a subsidiary of Softlogic Holdings PLC.
