##  Welcome to Asiri Health! 
With a mission to care for and improve the quality of human life, Asiri Health, Sri Lanka’s leading group of private hospitals & diagnostic facilities, offers some of the country’s most advanced clinical programs. 
Established in 1980, Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC is now the Asiri Health Group comprising seven strategically located hospitals – Asiri Medical, Asiri Surgical and Asiri Central Hospitals (in Colombo) and Asiri Hospitals Kandy, Galle and Matara - in three provinces and the country’s largest private laboratory network – Asiri Laboratories and a revolutionary new project in Port City – a state-of-the-art hospital to cater to an untapped medical tourism market.
With patient experience and positive outcomes always at the core of what we do, all our hospitals are accredited by either Joint Commission International (JCI) or Australian Council of Health Standards International (ACHSI) for patient care & safety, while our laboratories have all relevant industry-specific and quality management ISO certifications.
### Our Vision
To be a leading healthcare provider in South Asia with the highest quality of clinical standards.
### Our Mission
To care for and improve the quality of human life, through the provision of ethical healthcare solutions using cutting-edge technology.
### Our Values
  * Caring with a human touch.
  * Caring for society.
  * Caring for our employees.
  * Innovation and forward-focus.
  * Respect for all stakeholders.


# Our Reach
  * The Asiri Health Group now spans seven strategically located hospitals, including three in Colombo, two in Matara, one in Galle, and one in Kandy. 
  * Asiri Laboratories, our premier laboratory service, covers the entire spectrum of pathology with one main lab, six hospital labs, 17 satellite labs, and 100+ collection centres nationwide.
  * Conducting over 3,000 consultations and 14,500 tests daily, we have a capacity of over 800 beds across our hospitals.


### Quality and Safety
  * Our commitment to quality and safety is evident through our global accreditations: Joint Commission International (JCI) and Australian Council of Health Standards International (ACHSI).
  * Asiri Laboratories holds industry-specific and quality management ISO certifications.


### Our Team
  * Comprising some of the most dedicated, skilled, and experienced healthcare professionals, our team is the heart of Asiri Health.
  * Ongoing capacity building, training, and development keep our employees abreast of the latest medical practices and technology.


### Why Asiri?
  * Our name translates to 'a blessing,' reflecting our commitment to being your beacon for health and wellness.
  * Adhering to rigorous international standards, all our hospitals have international accreditations; Asiri Central Hospital holds the Joint Commission International’s Gold Seal of Approval, while Asiri Medical & Surgical Hospitals and Asiri Hospitals Kandy, Galle & Matara are accredited by the Australian Council on Healthcare Standards.
  * We offer the country’s most advanced, evidence-based clinical programs for treating extremely complex diseases, with a sound record of positive outcomes. 
  * Asiri Health also offers treatment for increasingly common lifestyle-based diseases, preventive healthcare, and the most complete menu of diagnostic tests.


## Future Outlook: Advancing Healthcare Horizons in Port City
In a pioneering move, Asiri Health is set to elevate healthcare standards by extending its reach to the vibrant Port City. This strategic expansion represents a significant milestone in our unwavering commitment to delivering top-notch healthcare services.
At the heart of our vision lies the construction of a cutting-edge hospital in Port City, a ground-breaking project poised to revolutionize the medical tourism landscape in Sri Lanka. Endorsed by government incentives, this initiative not only addresses a crucial gap in the market but also bolsters the nation's healthcare ecosystem. By attracting both local and international talent, we aim to create a hub of excellence that transcends borders.
Recognizing the vast potential of the healthcare market in Sri Lanka and its neighbouring regions, our venture is geared towards meeting the escalating demand for premium medical services. This ambitious project is not merely about fortifying our position in the healthcare industry; it is a dedicated effort to significantly contribute to the well-being of the nation.
As we embark on this promising journey, Asiri Health is poised to redefine healthcare standards, bridging gaps, and creating a legacy of excellence in medical services for generations to come.
