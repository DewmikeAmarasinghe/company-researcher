##  Company Profile 
Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC is engaged in providing healthcare and hospital services. The Company operates a hospital, provides healthcare services, and manages and holds investments in the healthcare sector. It has three operating segments. The Pre Care segment includes (outpatient department) OPD revenue, channeling revenue and OPD lab investigation services. The Post Care segment includes all (inpatient department) IPD revenue including inpatient drugs and lab investigation. The Pharmaceutical segment includes Pharmacy revenue. The Companyâ€™s Asiri Laboratories offer a range of tests, and encompasses approximately one main lab, six hospital labs, 17 satellite labs, and 70 collection centers across the island. The Company's hospital centers include Bone Marrow Transplant Unit, Urology Unit, Cosmetic Center, Asiri AOI Cancer Center, Stroke Unit, Brain and Spine Center, and Asiri Central Heart Center.
