## What is Home Health Care
Home health care is clinical medical care provided by skilled medical professionals, such as doctors and nurses, and is often prescribed as continuity of care following hospitalization. A doctor’s home visit may include diagnosis and treatment of illness(es) with periodic follow-up at home. It could also result in the patient being referred to a specialist consultant if necessary.
## Our Ambulances Service
Delivering Specialized Care
At Asiri Amazing Care, we prioritize your well-being, and our fleet of ambulances are designed to provide the highest standard of medical support. We offer both Basic Life Support (BLS) and Advanced Life Support (ALS).
  * Basic Life Support (BLS): Our BLS ambulances are equipped with a stretcher, providing essential support for various situations, including transportation to wards, consultations, or dialysis.
  * Advanced Life Support (ALS): For critical inter-ICU medi-evacuation warranting the utmost en-route critical medi-care, our ALS ambulances stand ready. These vehicles are equipped to simulate scenarios similar to an ICU, ETU or CCU.
