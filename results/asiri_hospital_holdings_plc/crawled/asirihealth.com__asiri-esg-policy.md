##  Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC’s Policy on Environment, Social and Governance (ESG) Sustainability 
At Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC, we recognize the critical role that healthcare institutions play in promoting not only the health of individuals but also the well-being of our communities and the environment. Our commitment to sustainability and responsible practices is integral to our mission of providing exceptional care. This policy outlines our approach to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) issues, reflecting our dedication to creating a positive impact on society and the planet.
  

##  Our Objective 
To be a leader in the healthcare industry by integrating sustainability and ethical practices into all aspects of our operations, ultimately contributing to a healthier environment and society.
## **Core Pillars**
  * **Environmental Stewardship:** Minimize our environmental footprint by reducing waste, conserving resources, and promoting clean energy.
  * **Social Responsibility:** Ensure that our operations benefit our patients, employees, and the wider community.
  * **Ethical Governance:** Uphold the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and accountability in our governance.

  

##  Goals of Core Pillars 
Environmental Stewardship
  * **Energy Efficiency:** Implement energy-saving measures and invest in renewable energy sources to reduce our carbon footprint.
  * **Waste Management:** Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials, and manage hazardous waste responsibly.
  * **Sustainable Procurement:** Source environmentally friendly products and services wherever possible.
  * **Water Conservation:** Employ water-saving technologies and practices to reduce water usage.

  

Social Responsibility
  * **Patient Care:** Deliver high-quality, equitable healthcare services to all patients.
  * **Community Engagement:** Collaborate with local organizations to address community health needs and promote wellness.
  * **Employee Well-being:** Foster a safe, inclusive, and supportive work environment for all staff.
  * **Diversity and Inclusion:** Promote diversity in our workforce and leadership, ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  * **Education and Training:** Provide continuous education and training to staff on sustainability and social responsibility.

  

Ethical Governance
  * **Compliance:** Adhere to all applicable laws, regulations, and ethical standards.
  * **Risk Management:** Identify, assess, and manage ESG-related risks in our operations.
  * **Transparency:** Communicate openly about our ESG initiatives, challenges, and progress.
  * **Stakeholder Engagement:** Engage with stakeholders to inform our ESG strategy and address concerns.
  * **Continuous Improvement:** Regularly review and update our ESG policy to reflect evolving best practices and stakeholder expectations.

  
  

##  Implementation 
The Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC’s Board Sustainability Committee will oversee the implementation of this policy, working closely with all departments to integrate ESG considerations into their activities. We will set measurable goals, track our progress, and report annually on our sustainability performance.
  

##  Conclusion 
Through this policy, Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC reaffirms its commitment to sustainability and responsible practices. We believe that by embracing our ESG responsibilities, we can provide better health outcomes, support our communities, and protect the environment for future generations.
