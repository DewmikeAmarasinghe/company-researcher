# BioClad Complete Works on Asiri Hospital, Sri Lanka
2,400m2 
of cladding installed on this project
125
bedded tertiary care hospital
200
staff working in a more hygienic environment
**Installer:** Brightfeed, Sri Lanka
**Client:** Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC, Sri Lanka
**Areas of Installation:** Operating Theatres, Catheterization Laboratory (Cath Lab), Intensive Care Unit (ICU), Central Sterile Services Department (CSSD), Accident & Emergency (A&E) and Endoscopy Unit. (2,400m²)
**Colours:** BioClad Pastel Mint & White
**Joint Detail:** Weld Joint
**Project Detail**
Private Healthcare Kings Asiri Health’s latest scheme is the 125 bed state of the art tertiary hospital located in Kandy, central Sri Lanka.
Infection control is a critical part of the ethos of Asiri and the requirement for antimicrobial technology runs throughout this most recent hospital.
BioClad Satin White and Pastel Mint antimicrobial systems, of which are impregnated by BioCote silver ion technology were specified by the client for internal wall linings.
Heat-welded joints and thermoformed corners were skilfully installed onto walls and ceilings by experienced Sri Lanka based and Bioclad approved installation team, Brightfeed International Ltd (Colombo) to give and easy to clean Class 0 fire rated wall lining in areas where hygiene is paramount.
