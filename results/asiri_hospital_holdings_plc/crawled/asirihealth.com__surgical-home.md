##### Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation
Offering an innovative, patient friendly facility run by a group of committed physiotherapists and rehabilitation specialists, Asiri Surgical Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation takes pleasure in what it has to offer. Our experienced and caring staff are here to help, whether your goal is to improve your general well-being, manage chronic pain, or recuperate from surgery. To restore strength, flexibility, and function, we design specialized rehabilitation programs using the most recent treatment approaches and modern tools. Beyond just treating symptoms, our comprehensive approach equips you with the skills and information necessary for long-term wellbeing. Our team is dedicated to helping you reach your specific recovery objectives, whether it be through hands-on therapy or customized exercises. Here at Asiri, we are certain that physiotherapy and rehabilitation may change your life and help you rediscover the joy of mobility. 
We provide,
  

  * Therapeutic exercises (strengthening, stretching and passive motion)
  * Joint mobilization & manipulation
  * Pre & post operative treatments & advice
  * Electrotherapy treatments
  * Suspension therapy
  * Lung function test
  * Physiotherapy for Rheumatologic conditions
  * Rehabilitation for Geriatric (Elderly Care) conditions 
  * Physiotherapy for Cardiorespiratory conditions
  * Rehabilitation for Sports Injuries

Our dental practices are staffed by highly skilled dentists and dental assistants who collaborate with periodontists, endodontists, and orthodontists to provide the best treatment possible for your individual needs.

##### Eye Care
Here at the Vision Excellence Centre at Asiri Surgical Hospital, we brighten the way to better, more vibrant vision. Our esteemed group of ophthalmologists and eye care specialists are committed to protecting and enhancing your priceless gift of sight. Modern diagnostic equipment and the latest developments are available in our centre to treat a variety of eye diseases, from simple vision correction to intricate surgical procedures. Whether you are considering laser eye surgery, need cataract treatment, or need a comprehensive eye exam, our skilled staff are committed to provide thoughtful, tailored care. We take great effort to ensure that you receive the best care possible for the health of your eyes. 
### _Our Services include,_
  * Comprehensive eye examinations to assess visual acuity and screen for potential eye disease. 
  * Vision correction & eye health - detect and manage common vision problems, refractive errors and other vision-related problems, prescribe corrective lenses and provide guidance on maintaining optimal eye health.
  * Cataract surgery
  * Glaucoma evaluation & treatment
  * Macular Degeneration treatment
  * Diabetic eye disease services
  * Treat Corneal Disorders


We prioritize patient comfort and safety, adhering to strict infection control protocols and maintaining a welcoming and soothing environment.
