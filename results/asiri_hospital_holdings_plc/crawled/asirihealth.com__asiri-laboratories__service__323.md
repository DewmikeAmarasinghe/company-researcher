# Genetic Laboratory
Home / Health Care Services / **Genetic Laboratory**
  
  
  

Established in 2005, the Genetic Laboratory of the Asiri Group offers the most comprehensive range of genetic and genomic tests in Sri Lanka. These tests encompass:
  * Traditional genetic tests for diagnosing chromosomal abnormalities and single gene disorders
  * Tests employing modern concepts such as pharmacogenomics, precision medicine, and exome sequencing
  * Pre- and post-test counseling services for patients
  * Comprehensive range of genetic tests for diagnosing infectious disorders
