Founded | Employees | CEO | Website

Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC operates hospitals and provides healthcare service in Sri Lanka. It operates through Pre Care, Post Care, Pharmaceutical, and Other segments. The company operates centers, including heart, brain and spine, blood cancer and bone marrow transplant, stroke, interventional radiology, and kidney transplant unit centers, as well as urology, mother and baby care, cancer, and IVF and fertility centers; and mindfulness, vaccination, wellness, and community centers.
