## 2. Business Model and Revenue

- **Business model**
  - Integrated private healthcare group operating **six hospitals** (Asiri Central, Asiri Surgical, Asiri Medical in Colombo; Asiri Kandy, Galle, Matara) and the country’s largest private laboratory network, with a future state-of-the-art hospital in **Port City (2027)** aimed at expanding medical tourism.
  - Centers of Excellence and 24/7 Emergency services across facilities, with international accreditation (JCI, ACHSI) and ISO certifications for laboratories.
  - Broad clinical footprint across key units: heart, brain and spine, blood cancer & bone marrow transplant, stroke, interventional radiology, kidney transplant, urology, mother & baby care, cancer, IVF, mindfulness, vaccination, wellness.

- **Revenue streams**
  - Pre Care: OPD revenue, channeling revenue, OPD laboratory investigations.
  - Post Care: IPD revenue, inpatient drugs and laboratory investigations.
  - Pharmaceutical: Pharmacy revenue.
  - Diagnostics: Asiri Laboratories with one main lab, six hospital labs, 17 satellite labs, 70 collection centers; capacity for high test volume.
  - Service mix and capacity: **3500+ consultations per day**, **4250+ tests offered**, **14,500+ tests per day**, over **800 beds** across hospitals.
  - Insurance/discount channels: Asiri Agrahara (discounts/privileges for state sector employees) and Asiri Suraksha (government-funded student insurance).

- **Pricing/access strategies**
  - Price access via government and employee insurance schemes and related discounts; government-linked student insurance program; ongoing online services (payment portal, online pharmacy) to streamline billing. 

- **Financial scale (context)**
  - Revenue (TTM) around **LKR 33.24 billion**; net income around **LKR 3.70 billion**; market cap ~**LKR 37.1 billion**.

## 18. Challenges and Opportunities

- Investment and facility
  - **US$100 million** hospital to be built by a wholly owned subsidiary, **Asiri Port City Hospital Ltd**, in the Colombo Port City SEZ.
  - Projected to have **500 rooms**; lease site for **99 years** with Colombo Port City Economic Commission.
  - Designed to deliver state-of-the-art healthcare with integrated clinical practice, education and research; medical team drawn from local and international experts; will engage an unnamed international partner for strategic/operational consulting.
  - Part of the **Asiri Hospital Holdings PLC** network and the **Softlogic Group**; aims to attract medical tourism and revamp Sri Lanka’s healthcare sector.

- Regulatory and strategic incentives
  - Cabinet approval to designate the Port City Hospital project as a **Primary Business of Strategic Importance**, enabling exemptions/incentives under Colombo Port City regulations (Regulations No 2 of 2023).

- Market context and opportunities
  - Port City Colombo is a multi-currency SEZ; initiative targets addressing Sri Lanka’s healthcare sector challenges such as medicine shortages and staff shortages, contributing to a potential rise in international medical travel.

- HealthTech ecosystem collaboration
  - **Asiri Health & ICTA** partnership to accelerate HealthTech startups; announced during Global Entrepreneurship Week 2025.
  - Seven HealthTech startups showcased; Asiri Health provided access to senior leadership, live hospital environments, and clinical/commercial feedback to validate solutions and enable scaling.
  - Plan to establish an annual HealthTech startup showcase; long-term aim to pilot, partner and scale solutions within Asiri’s hospital network.

- Medicare 2025 sponsorship
  - **Platinum Sponsor** for Medicare 2025 (Feb 28–Mar 2, 2025, BMICH, Colombo).
  - Highlights: Centers of Excellence in **Neurosurgery (Brain & Spine)**, **Cardiac Surgery (Heart)**, and **Oncology**; renowned IVF programme; governance for the new **Asiri Academy of Health Sciences**; Bone Marrow Transplant and Fertility Centre; representation for Asiri Laboratories.
