## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- Business model
  - Integrated private healthcare group operating six hospitals across Colombo Kandy Galle Matara with the country’s largest private laboratory network
  - Port City Port City Hospital planned for 2027 as part of medical tourism and regional healthcare expansion
  - Centers of Excellence and 24/7 Emergency services across facilities with international accreditation
  - Broad clinical footprint spanning heart brain and spine blood cancer and bone marrow transplant stroke interventional radiology kidney transplant urology mother & baby care cancer IVF mindfulness vaccination wellness
- Revenue streams
  - Pre Care: OPD revenue channeling OPD laboratory investigations
  - Post Care: IPD revenue inpatient drugs and laboratory investigations
  - Pharmaceutical: Pharmacy revenue
  - Diagnostics: Asiri Laboratories with 1 main lab 6 hospital labs 17 satellite labs 70 collection centers
  - Service mix and capacity: **3500+ consultations per day**; **4250+ tests offered**; **14,500+ tests per day**; over **800 beds**
  - Insurance/discount channels: Asiri Agrahara discounts for state sector employees; Asiri Suraksha government-funded student insurance
- Pricing/access strategies
  - Government and employee insurance schemes along with online services to streamline billing including payment portal and online pharmacy
