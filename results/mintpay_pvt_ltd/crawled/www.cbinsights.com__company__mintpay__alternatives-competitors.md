## Compare Mintpay to Competitors
S
Split
Split operates in the financial technology sector and focuses on providing Shariah-compliant buy-now-pay-later services. The company's main offering is a payment platform that allows customers to shop and pay in interest-free installments at various stores, with the added benefit of no late fees or other charges. Split primarily serves the e-commerce industry. It was founded in 2018 and is based in Singapore.
SplitIt
SplitIt provides buy now, pay later (BNPL) solutions within the financial technology sector. The company offers a merchant-branded Installments-as-a-Service platform that facilitates buy now, pay later (BNPL) at the point of sale, allowing consumers to pay in installments while earning credit card rewards. Its services address the limitations of traditional buy now, pay later (BNPL) providers by offering a network of application programming interfaces (API) for card networks, issuers, and acquirers. SplitIt was formerly known as PayItSimple. It was founded in 2012 and is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
H
Hello Clever
Hello Clever provides a payment gateway for instant payments and cashback rewards within the financial technology sector. The company facilitates transactions and offers cashback for customers, while businesses experience fund settlements. Hello Clever's solutions integrate with e-commerce platforms and include fraud protection. It was founded in 2021 and is based in Sydney, Australia.
Tala
Tala provides digital financial services. The company offers a money app that facilitates access to credit, payments, savings, and transfers, utilizing artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning to create financial experiences. Tala primarily serves individuals seeking services beyond traditional banking. It was founded in 2011 and is based in Santa Monica, California.
Oriente
Oriente operates as a financial technology (fintech) company. It provides identity-first digital solutions. The company offers services including artificial intelligence (AI) driven credit scoring, on-demand lending, and point-of-sale (POS) financing. It serves the fintech sector and utilizes big data analytics and machine learning to deliver financial services. It was founded in 2017 and is based in Hong Kong.
The Very Group
The Very Group operates in the online retail sector, offering a variety of brands and payment options. The company provides a platform for consumers to purchase a range of products, including fashion, home goods, and electronics, with different payment plans available. The Very Group primarily caters to individual consumers seeking retail options. The Very Group was formerly known as Shop Direct. It was founded in 2003 and is based in Liverpool, United Kingdom.
