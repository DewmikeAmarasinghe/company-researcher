####  Mintpay
**Own your shopping freedom**
#### Short Description
Sri Lanka's first buy now, pay later payment facility that aims to boost the local economy. 
#### Long Description
Mintpay allows shoppers to spread their cost responsibly thereby aiding local merchants to drive conversions and increase. The decisioning engine of Mintpay is powered by machine learning and data analytics to determine e-KYC and mitigate risk.
