### Frequently Asked Questions
What are Mint Coins?
Mint Coins are rewards you earn on every Mintpay transaction; whether you Pay Now, Pay Later, or convert a Mintpay Voucher. Use them across 2,500+ partner stores or to pay off your pending instalments.
How do l earn Mint Coins?
Earn Mint Coins every time you use Mintpay-whether you Pay Now, Pay Later, convert a Voucher, or settle instalments early.
How can l use my Mint Coins?
At the point of checkout, toggle "Use Mint Coins" enter the amount, and confirm. You can also use coins to settle Pay Later instalments.
Do Mint Coins expire?
No, your Mint coins don't expire. Use them whenever you're ready.
Is there a limit to how many Mint Coins I can collect?
There's no maximum amount. Collect as much as you like, as often as you like.
Can I use Mint Coins with other discounts?
Yes, you can use your Mint Coins alongside ongoing discounts and offers by stacking them up for even more rewards.
