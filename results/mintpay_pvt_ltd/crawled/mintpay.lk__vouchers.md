### Frequently Asked Questions
What are Mintpay Vouchers?
Mintpay Vouchers are digital gift vouchers that offer the perfect way to Gift Better. Recipients can choose from thousands of stores and millions of gifts, making it a truly personalized gift.
Where can I purchase Mintpay Vouchers?
You can purchase Mintpay Vouchers directly from our website or through the Mintpay app. Refer to this link for instructions on how to purchase a voucher within the app.
Where can I redeem the voucher?
You can redeem your Mintpay Voucher at all Mintpay partner merchants, both online and in-store. Whether you're shopping, dining, or traveling, enjoy the flexibility to use your voucher across a wide range of stores and services. Refer to this link for instructions on how to add and redeem the voucher in the Mintpay app.
Can I give a physical Mintpay Voucher?
Yes, you can. After purchasing the Mintpay Voucher online, you can download the voucher PDF, which can be printed and given to the recipient. The recipient can scan the QR code in the PDF using the Mintpay app to add the voucher to their Mintpay account. Refer to this link for instructions on how to send and receive a physical Mintpay Voucher.
How long are Mintpay Vouchers valid?
Mintpay Vouchers are valid for one year from the date of issue. However, you can convert them to Mint Coins and keep them indefinitely as long as your account is active.
Can I use a Mintpay Voucher for more than one purchase?
No, a Mintpay Voucher must be used in a single purchase. However, you can convert it to Mint Coins, which allows you to choose how much to redeem across multiple purchases.
