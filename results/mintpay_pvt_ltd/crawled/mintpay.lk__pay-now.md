### Frequently Asked Questions
What is Pay Now?
Pay Now lets you pay the full amount upfront using your debit or credit card and get up to 10% Cashback as Mint Coins.
How do I get Cashback?
With Mintpay's Pay Now option, you’ll earn Cashback as Mint Coins after completing your payment. 1.00 Mint Coin = LKR 1.00, redeemable on your subsequent Mintpay purchase.
Can I get Cashback on all Pay Now purchases?
Yes, you'll earn Cashback as Mint Coins on all Pay Now purchases across all Mintpay partner stores.
Can I stack Cashback on top of other discounts?
Yes, you can stack the Cashback value on top of an existing Mintpay discount to maximize your savings.
Is there a minimum order value to get Cashback?
No minimum order value is required to receive Cashback as Mint Coins.
Is there a maximum limit on the amount I can receive through Pay Now?
There's no maximum limit, you can receive any amount using Pay Now.
