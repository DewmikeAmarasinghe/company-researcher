# Delivering holistic payment solutions
Mint Payments enables businesses to do what they do best, by providing an end-to-end payments solution that adds value & makes business easy. At Mint, we work with the best technology, security, partners, and people to develop products & features that enable merchants to accept and make payments.  
  
By bringing payments capabilities, features, and pricing usually reserved for enterprise companies to businesses of all sizes, Mint works meet all our merchant's transaction needs and enable greater possibilities for under-served industries.
