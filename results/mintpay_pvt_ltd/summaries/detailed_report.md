## 2. Business Model and Revenue

- **Business model**
  - **Sri Lanka BNPL platform** mintpay (PVT) LTD, founded **2020**; offers at online and in-store purchases.
  - **Pay Later**: split into 3 interest-free instalments over 2 months; first instalment due at purchase.
  - **Pay Now**: full upfront payment at checkout; eligible orders earn cashback in **Mint Coins**.
  - **Merchant ecosystem**: integrated with popular e-commerce platforms; **2,500+ Active Partner Merchants**; gateway supports online and physical stores.
  - **Merchant payments**: merchants are paid “weekly paid in full and upfront”; Mintpay handles the remainder processes.
  - **Growth signals**: over **500,000+ Sri Lankans** use Mintpay; partnerships with brands/buy-now-pay-later advocates; basket value and frequency boosts reported by partners.

- **Revenue streams / pricing strategy**
  - **Late fees (Pay Later)**: **LKR 500** late fee if due payment not received, plus **LKR 300** after 7 days and another **LKR 300** after 14 days; cap: up to **LKR 2,200** per order.
  - **Fees for Mintpay use**: charges or fees described in **Schedule 1** (specific amounts not disclosed here).
  - **Pay Later split limits**: initial split limit may start small and increase with repayment behavior; higher upfront option possible when original order value exceeds the approved limit (Higher Upfront Payment).
  - **Pay Now incentives**: cashback rewarded as Mint Coins up to **10%** of eligible Pay Now purchases; Mint Coins valued at **1 LKR** each and retrievable for future transactions.
  - **Mint Coins & vouchers**: Mint Coins can be redeemed to pay all or part of Pay Later/Pay Now balances; vouchers are non-refundable but may be converted to Mint Coins; Mint Coins expire only with inactivity (1 year) or account termination.

## 3. Products and Services

- Pay options
  - Pay Now
    - Full payment upfront with debit/credit card
    - Up to **10% Cashback** as Mint Coins
  - Pay Later
    - Split into **3 interest-free instalments**
    - No interest or extra fees
    - Early payments earn extra Mint Coins
    - Split limit policy: can exceed the limit if there are no pending payments; first instalment due at purchase
- Mint Coins
  - Rewards earned on every Mintpay transaction
  - Use at **2,500+ partner stores**
  - Mint Coins never expire
  - 1 Mint Coin = Rs. 1
  - Can be used to settle Pay Later instalments
  - Can be stacked with ongoing discounts
- Mintpay Vouchers
  - Digital gift vouchers redeemable at **2,500+ stores** (online & in-store)
  - Can be converted to Mint Coins for use across multiple purchases
  - Voucher validity: 1 year from issue
  - Mint Coins from vouchers have no expiry when converted
  - Non-transferable; single-purchase use unless converted
  - Physical vouchers available; personalized messages supported
- App & usage
  - Instant account verification; manage payments; track Mint Coins and purchases
  - Shop online or in-store with Mintpay; scan to download the app; mint coins at checkout
- Network & scope
  - Access at over **2,500 leading stores/brands** across electronics, food & beverage, lifestyle, etc.

## 4. Company History

- Founding and platform
  - Mintpay is a Sri Lanka-based BNPL platform launched in the latter part of **2020**.
  - Co-founders: **Urmila Chandrasekaran** (CEO) and **Kukaraj Tharmasegaram** (CTO).

## 12. Technology Stack

- Platform and access
  - `Mintpay Platforms` include the website `mintpay.lk` and mobile apps for **iOS** and **Android**; sign-up in under 2 minutes with no verification and no minimum order value.
  - Partners: over **2,500** Mintpay partner stores online and in-store.
- Payment options and rewards
  - `Pay Now` – pay full upfront and earn up to **10% Mint Coins** cashback; Mint Coins redeemable on future purchases.
  - `Pay Later` – split into **3 interest-free installments over 2 months**; no interest/fees; early settlement earns extra Mint Coins; automatic payments from linked card; split limit may increase with use.
  - Mint Coins – valued at **1 Mint Coin = LKR 1.00**; no expiry unless 1 year of inactivity or account termination; can be used to pay future purchases or settle pending installments.
  - Vouchers – Mintpay vouchers redeemable; can be converted to Mint Coins; vouchers are one-time use and non-refundable; if purchase exceeds voucher value, pay the remainder via Pay Now or Pay Later.
- Infrastructure and payments ecosystem
  - Integrated with `DFCC Bank Internet Payment Gateway` for BNPL and Pay Now transactions.
  - Card networks involved include `Visa` and `MasterCard` with pre-authorizations as part of approval.
  - Merchant portal supports BNPL offerings; store directory shows merchant participation and cashback terms.
- Data, privacy, and security
  - Cookies, IP tracking, device identifiers; analytics via `Google Analytics` and `Amplitude`.
  - Identity verification (KYC) using NIC/Passport; 3D Secure or equivalent for online payments; privacy policy governs data handling and sharing with partners.
- Legal and governance
  - Terms & Conditions governed by Sri Lankan law; arbitration in Colombo; complaints handled via support channels.

## 24. Pricing Information

- **Pay Now**
  - Full upfront payment with debit/credit card; **up to 10% Cashback** as Mint Coins.
  - Mint Coins: value **1 Mint Coin = Rs 1**; earned on Pay Now purchases; can be used on future Mintpay purchases; coins do not expire while account active.
  - Mint Coins can be used alongside discounts at 2,500+ partner stores.

- **Pay Later**
  - Split into **3 interest-free instalments over 2 months**; first instalment at purchase, remaining two monthly.
  - No interest or fees for using the service; late fees apply if payments are missed: ** Rs 500** initial, **Rs 300** after 7 days, **Rs 300** after 14 days (max **Rs 2,200** per order).
  - Split limit: initial credit shown per order; can increase with repayment behavior; paying instalments early can raise the limit; option for higher upfront payment if order value exceeds limit.

- Mint Coins & Vouchers
  - Mint Coins earned via Pay Now, Pay Later, early settlements, vouchers, or referrals; redeemable toward Pay Now or Pay Later purchases.
  - Mint Coins value: Rs 1 each; non-transferable; not redeemable for cash; redeem across 2,500+ stores.
  - Mintpay Vouchers: digital or physical; valid 1 year; non-refundable; can convert to Mint Coins; redeemable at Mintpay partner stores.

- Pricing example (Sri Lanka)
  - Rs 12,000 purchase example: Pay Later as 3x Rs 4,000 (today + 30 days + 60 days); early payments earn extra Mint Coins (e.g., Rs 120 per early instalment); Pay Now shows cashback in Mint Coins as applicable.

- Partnerships and scope
  - 2,500+ partner stores; Lans.lk integration; Seylan Bank BNPL partnership launched on **1 March 2021**.
