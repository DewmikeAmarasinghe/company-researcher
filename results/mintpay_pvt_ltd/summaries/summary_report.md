## 3. Business Model and Revenue
- **Business model overview**
  - Sri Lanka BNPL platform offering both online and in-store purchases
  - **Pay Later**: 3 installments over 2 months; **Pay Now**: full upfront payment with cashback
  - Merchant ecosystem: 2,500+ active partner merchants; integration with online platforms and physical stores
  - Merchants are paid weekly upfront; Mintpay handles remainder processing
- **Revenue streams / pricing structure**
  - Late fees (Pay Later): Rs 500 on due date, Rs 300 after 7 days, Rs 300 after 14 days; cap per order Rs 2,200
  - Fees for Mintpay usage detailed in Schedule 1 (specific amounts not disclosed)
  - Pay Later split limits adjust with repayment behavior; Higher Upfront Payment possible when order value exceeds approved limit
  - Pay Now incentives include Cashback; Mint Coins value at 1 Mint Coin = Rs 1
- **Growth signals**
  - Basket value uplift up to ~30% and ~50% higher purchase frequency reported by partner merchants
  - Mintpay positioning emphasizes rapid approval and merchant-friendly settlements
- **Customer value proposition**
  - Consumers gain flexibility with installment financing and upfront rewards
  - Merchants benefit from expanded baskets and higher frequency

-Notes
- Mint Coins and vouchers are central to the value loop and cross-store redemption

## 4. Products and Services
- **Pay options**
  - Pay Now
    - Full payment upfront with debit/credit card
    - Up to **10% Cashback** as Mint Coins
    - Mint Coins redeemable on future Mintpay purchases
  - Pay Later
    - Split into **3 interest-free instalments**
    - First instalment due at purchase; remaining payments scheduled over ~2 months
    - Early payments earn extra Mint Coins; late payments incur fees
- **Mint Coins**
  - Rewards earned on every Mintpay transaction
  - Use at **2,500+ partner stores**
  - Mint Coins never expire; 1 Mint Coin = Rs. 1
  - Can be used to settle Pay Later balances
  - Can be combined with ongoing discounts
- **Mintpay Vouchers**
  - Digital gift vouchers redeemable at **2,500+ stores** (online & in-store)
  - Can be converted to Mint Coins; validity: 1 year from issue
  - Non-transferable; single-purchase use unless converted; physical vouchers available
- **App & usage**
  - Instant account verification; manage payments; track Mint Coins and purchases
  - Shop online or in-store; scan to download the app; earn Mint Coins at checkout
- **Network & scope**
  - Access at over **2,500 leading stores/brands** across electronics, food & beverage, lifestyle, etc.

-Notes
- Architecture leverages Algoredge for AI-driven decisioning; integrates with `DFCC Bank Internet Payment Gateway` for BNPL and Pay Now

## 12. Pricing Information
- **Pay Now**
  - Full upfront payment with debit/credit card
  - Up to **10% Cashback** as Mint Coins
  - Mint Coins valued at 1 Mint Coin = Rs 1; coins redeemable for future purchases
  - Coins can be used alongside discounts at 2,500+ stores
- **Pay Later**
  - 3 installments over 2 months; first instalment due at purchase
  - No interest or fees; late fees apply if payments are missed
  - Late fee schedule: Rs 500 on due date; Rs 300 after 7 days; Rs 300 after 14 days; maximum Rs 2,200 per order
  - Split limit may increase with repayment behavior; higher upfront option if original order value exceeds limit
- **Mint Coins & Vouchers**
  - Mint Coins earned on every Mintpay transaction; redeemable for Pay Now or Pay Later purchases
  - Mint Coins are non-transferable and non-cash redeemable; minted vouchers are 1-year valid
  - Vouchers can be converted to Mint Coins; Mint Coins from vouchers do not expire when converted
- **Practical example**
  - Rs 12,000 purchase to Pay Later: 3x Rs 4,000 payments; early repayments earn Mint Coins (e.g., Rs 120 per early instalment)
  - Pay Now provides cashback in Mint Coins; Mint Coins can be used in future transactions
- **Scope and reach**
  - Mintpay operates with **2,500+ partner stores** including Lans.lk integration and Seylan Bank BNPL partnership

-Notes
- Pricing is designed to encourage faster repayment, reward upfront payments, and promote continued use through Mint Coins
