# Digital Mobility Solutions Lanka Company Description
Digital Mobility Solutions Lanka PLC, a technological company, develops and operates mobility-related software in Sri Lanka.
Its PickMe software facilitates a real-time connection between various stakeholders requiring mobility solutions, including independent third-party drivers, merchants, and consumers; and ride-hailing, parcel delivery, and food and grocery delivery, as well as on-demand trucking services.
The company was incorporated in 2015 and is headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka.
