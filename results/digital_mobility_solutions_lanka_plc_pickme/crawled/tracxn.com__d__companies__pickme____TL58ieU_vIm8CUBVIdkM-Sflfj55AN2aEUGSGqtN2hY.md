## PickMe - About the company
PickMe is a seed company based in Colombo (Sri Lanka), founded in 2015 by Jiffry Zulfer. It operates as an App-based platform offering taxi-hailing and on-demand delivery services. PickMe has raised $2.5M in funding from IFC. The company has 11 active competitors, including 1 that is funded. Its top competitors include companies like YOGO, FlitWays and JRide. 
### Company Details
App-based platform offering taxi-hailing and on-demand delivery services. The platform enables users to find and book their rides by providing location, passenger details, etc. It offers features like live vehicle tracking, online payments, etc. The mobile app is available for Android, Windows, and iOS platform.
