## Corporate Overview
PickMe is the official brand of Digital Mobility Solutions Lanka PLC., a software service provider, founded in June 2015.  
  
Since our inception, PickMe has reshaped the landscape of local transportation through advanced mobility technology.   
  
We have enabled a record 200 million+ journeys, covering over 900 million kilometers and processed over Rs. 100 billion in transactions with the power of the technology we developed.   
  
Our commitment to innovation, investments in technology and delightful user experience has propelled us to become a cornerstone of everyday life in Sri Lanka.
### Our Core Purpose
## Joyful Mobility For A Better Life
PickMe envisions the intelligent upgrade of mobility, consequently inspiring Sri Lanka towards matching global standards in effective problem solving via technology.   
  
PickMe is committed to innovate and build solutions that push boundaries in the mobility space using advanced technology and Big Data.   
  
Our mission is to upgrade the efficiency, reliability, and safety of the country's mobility services by introducing technology, standards, order, and convenience into the system.
