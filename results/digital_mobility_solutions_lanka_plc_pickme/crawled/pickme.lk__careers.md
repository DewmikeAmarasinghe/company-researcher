## Life At PickMe
Proactive, optimistic yet pragmatic entrepreneurship is at the heart of PickMe’s work culture because these values led to – and continue to lead to – our ground-breaking technology.  
  
PickMe’s work culture pulsates with excitement at the challenges posed to them. Our open office is a hive of collaboration where some of the smartest minds in tech debate and design alongside veteran Engineers, transport logisticians, technicians, data scientists and other professionals.  
  
The work of these men and women ensure that the PickMe platform reaches its fullest potential and it is – not a mere ride-hailing app, but a community dedicated to solving the transport riddle for the benefit of drivers, customers and merchants.  

## Our Values 
Our values provide fundamental value and importance and are essential and lasting tenets of our organization. They form the guiding principles for our actions and decisions. Our values give us a sense of belonging and togetherness that in turn drives better company performance.
### We Imagine, We Innovate, And We Never Give Up
We question and challenge the status quo and constantly imagine what more can be done and what can be done better through innovation and technology. We will never give up on our journey of bringing better solutions of value to drive joy in our society.
### We Are Hungry To Be Better
We are driven and strive to keep improving ourselves. We are not complacent because of our success and work with a growth mindset for continuous development and improvement. We learn from our mistakes and keep moving forward swiftly.
### Trust Is Given, Respect It
We have faith and confidence in you and your abilities to do your job and we expect you to maintain the trust placed in you. We hold ourselves responsible and accountable for our actions.
### We Do The Right Thing, Always 
We always act in the company’s best interest and work in a legal, moral, honest, decent and reliable manner. We do not act in ways that question our credibility or integrity.
### We Give A Hand Up, Take It On
We encourage you to reach your full potential. We provide the environment and opportunities for learning and development and expect you to work hard to succeed on your own merit.
