## 12. Technology Stack

- Core platforms and approach
  - **CPaaS** with *multi-operator support* enabling access to multiple telecom operators; ecosystem includes **mSpace** for apps via Telecom APIs.
  - **Sprout**: enterprise AI agent for automated customer interactions across channels.
  - Global reach with offices in Singapore, USA, Bangladesh, Philippines, and Sri Lanka.

- In-house telecom infrastructure
  - **SIGTRAN Gateway** (built in-house) to bridge SS7 signaling over IP; eliminates third-party stacks.
  - In-house **SIGTRAN Stack** supporting `SCTP`, `M3UA`, and `MTP3` with native protocol conversion.
  - Deployment options: on-premise, cloud, or hybrid; supports high availability and geo-redundancy.
  - **SMSC** with elastic, clustered architecture for on-demand scaling; overload protection; GUI-based configurable routing (`M3UA`, `SCTP`, `MTP3`).

- Messaging and location services
  - **SMSC** features: overload protection, routing rules, DND module with REST API; handles high SMS traffic with minimal downtime.
  - **LBS** (Location-based Services): query-based, open platform with `MLP` APIs for third-party VAS; supports location data via SMS/USSD.

- AI and data capabilities
  - Tailored AI solutions across functions: **Customer Support**, **Sales**, **Recruitment**, **Marketing & Experience**, **Operations & Supply Chain**, **HR & Internal Support**.
  - Reported outcomes: **70% reduction** in manual operations; **40+ enterprise clients**; **100% customized AI workflows**; PhD-led data science team; GDPR/compliance alignment.

- Documentation and resources
  - Data sheets and whitepapers available (e.g., Sigtran Gateway, Ansible/OS migration, Data Science & AI/ML).
