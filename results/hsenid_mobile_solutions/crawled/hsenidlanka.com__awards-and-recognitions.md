> **Most Outstanding Contribution Award – 2012**
> The National Best Quality ICT Awards (NBQSA) which was previously known as the National Best Quality Software Awards recognizes the outstanding achievements of individuals and organizations in Sri Lanka who/which have contributed to the development of ICT to create an opportunity gain international recognition
The Most Outstanding Contribion Award is presented to a very special ICT Leader in the country. BCS Sri Lankan section has partnered with the ICT Agency of Sri Lanka since 2009 in presenting this award. The award is presented to a person for his/her unique and yeoman services contributed to the industry for over 25 years and for playing a significant role in shaping the current ICT indystry landscape of Sri Lanka
