## Enterprise Case Studies 
#### Sentiment Analysis 
for Restaurant Aggregator
##  Challenge · Solution · Impact 
## Challenge
A US restaurant aggregator had messy support ticket handling, causing slow replies especially for angry or negative customer cases. 
## Solution
AI sentiment analysis with tagging auto-detected negative tone and prioritized critical tickets for quicker escalation. 
## Impact
Ticket triaging became automated, resolution speed improved by about 25%, and customer satisfaction increased. 
#### Customer Segmentation &
Targeting
##  Challenge · Solution · Impact 
## Challenge
A large bank needed to split millions of customers into four clear tiers based on product usage and engagement, so marketing could be more precise and effective. 
## Solution
A dynamic segmentation model was introduced, re-checking customer behavior every quarter and assigning tier-based benefits, including exclusive offers for premium clients. 
## Impact
The bank saw stronger engagement, a 50% jump in new account openings, and meaningful overall business growth. 
#### Data Warehousing for Restaurant Aggregator
##  Challenge · Solution · Impact 
## Challenge
A US restaurant aggregator had fragmented sales and promo data across thousands of outlets, making it hard to see customer behavior clearly. 
## Solution
Implemented a centralized data warehouse to combine fragmented sales and customer data into one real-time, insight-ready view. 
## Impact
Reporting delays dropped by 70%, campaigns performed better, and merchants gained stronger customer base visibility. 
#### Turning Failures into Revenue Optimization
##  Challenge · Solution · Impact 
## Challenge
Over 50% of daily charging requests failed due to low balances and connector overloads, delaying revenue for a telco handling 10M subscribers and 3M mandates/day. 
## Solution
A ML “Propensity to Collect” model was used to score subscribers from recent charging data and charge high-likelihood users first, reducing failures and accelerating revenue. 
## Impact
Charges succeeded more often, revenue was captured earlier in the cycle, and daily liquidity improved. 
#### Predictive 
Churn Prevention
##  Challenge · Solution · Impact 
## Challenge
The operator was losing 7% of users monthly and couldn’t spot churn early, leading to revenue loss. 
## Solution
An AI churn model analyzed reload and usage patterns to flag high-risk customers ahead of time. 
## Impact
Early detection enabled targeted campaigns, reducing churn and boosting retention and lifetime value.
