## About hSenid
hSenid specializes in human resource (HR) management software solutions across various sectors. The company offers cloud-based and on-premise HR information systems, time and attendance tracking, and HR outsourcing services. hSenid primarily serves sectors that require comprehensive HR and payroll solutions, such as education, agriculture, and financial services. It was founded in 1997 and is based in Colombo, Sri Lanka.
