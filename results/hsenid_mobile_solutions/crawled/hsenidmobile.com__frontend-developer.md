#### Frontend Developer (AI Focus)
# Who We Are
hSenid Mobile Solutions is an international powerhouse in innovative technology, with a proud 28-year legacy. With offices in Singapore, USA, Bangladesh, Philippines, and Sri Lanka, we empower businesses globally with cutting-edge solutions. Our product portfolio includes Sprout, an AI Sales Agent that redefines sales efficiency for industries like banking, education, eCommerce, real estate, and more.  
  
  
We’re looking for a curious and passionate Frontend Developer who loves building clean, easy-to-use web pages, applications and who’s excited to work with AI. If you’re eager to learn, grow, and bring fresh ideas, we want you on our team!
# Key Responsibilities
  * Build web pages and apps that look great and work well on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  * Work closely with designers, product managers, marketers, and backend engineers.
  * Write clean, easy-to-read code and join code reviews.
  * Make sure our sites and applications load fast and run smoothly.
  * Help shape how our frontend code is organized.
  * Keep up with new AI tools and share insights with the team!


# Required Skills and Experience
  * **AI Curious:** You’re excited to work with AI tools and make web and application development better (AI tools: V0, Motiff, Cursor, and Bolt New).
  * **Hungry to Learn:** You ask questions, try new things, and always look for ways to get better.
  * **Tech-Savvy:** You have 1–2 years of experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and a modern framework (React, Vue, or Angular).
  * **API-Friendly:** You know how to connect your front end with backend services.
  * **Good Communicator:** You explain your ideas clearly and listen to feedback.
  * **Problem Solver:** You enjoy fixing bugs and making things smoother for users.
  * **Detail-Oriented:** You care about how things look and feel to the user.
  * You have a degree or diploma in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  * **Design Thinking is a plus!**
