# Union Assurance and oDoc inks deal to enhance healthcare services
by Gayan Abeykoon June 10, 2024
June 10, 2024 1:06 am   
  

_38_
Union Assurance, announced a partnership with oDoc, a premier telemedicine company in Sri Lanka. Recognising the need for convenient and efficient healthcare services, Union Assurance is steadfast in its commitment to improving access to healthcare for its policyholders through strategic partnerships and technological innovation.
Sri Lanka travel guide
This partnership offers significant benefits to both Union Assurance and oDoc customers. Union Assurance policyholders will receive exclusive discounts on all doctor consultations via the oDoc platform, easily accessible through the Union Assurance ClickLife App. This exclusive offer ensures seamless access to quality healthcare, making it more convenient for policyholders to connect with healthcare professionals.

Additionally, Union Assurance will provide customised Life Insurance solutions to oDoc customers. Expert financial advisors from Union Assurance will offer personalized guidance, helping oDoc customers secure their financial futures with solutions tailored to their unique needs and preferences.
Union Assurance Chief Partnership Distribution Officer Wathsala Aluthgedara stated; “At Union Assurance, we strive to elevate the Life Insurance experience for our policyholders. As healthcare costs continue to rise, we are committed to providing accessible and comprehensive solutions to our policyholders and this strategic partnership with oDoc underscores this focus. By integrating oDoc’s advanced telemedicine platform with our Life Insurance expertise, we are set to make a significant positive impact on our policyholders’ lives.”
oDoc Chief Executive Officer Lahiru Karunarathna said; “We are thrilled to partner with Union Assurance to expand access to quality healthcare services across the island. Through this collaboration, we aim to leverage our technology and expertise to improve health outcomes of our communities.”
Union Assurance continues to redefine the Life Insurance landscape with its pioneering self-service ClickLife App. This ecosystem is designed to provide optimal value to users, allowing them to manage their Life Insurance policies effortlessly via smart devices, anytime and anywhere. The ClickLife App eliminates the need for paperwork and offers a range of innovative and new-age features, all aimed at enriching the well-being of its users.
