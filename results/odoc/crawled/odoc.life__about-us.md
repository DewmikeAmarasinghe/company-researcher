## WHO WE ARE
# About Us
We are proud to be Sri Lanka’s largest telemedicine company, covering over 200,000 lives across Sri Lanka, India and the Maldives. We believe having great healthcare only improves peoples’ lives for the better which is why everyone should have easy access to it. We launched in 2017 with the primary goal of making healthcare easily accessible for all Sri Lankans by providing high quality care online from the best doctors the country has to offer. Our mission which we strive for everyday is to ensure that exceptional healthcare is accessible, affordable and truly personal to all.
#### Who are the doctors on oDoc?
Both general practitioners and specialist doctors can be found on oDoc. If you are a registered doctor and would like to channel on oDoc, please get in touch with us!
