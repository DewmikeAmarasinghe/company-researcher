## HealthTech startups in Sri Lanka

## 1. oDoc
oDoc offers a mobile application that enables consumers to connect with a doctor remotely and avail teleconsultation for non-emergency conditions. The SaaS-based mobile application also allows consumers to request or schedule an in-person consultation, compare doctor profiles, consult anonymously, search for health information including disease symptoms, and store health records.
