## Basic Information
We are building Emerging Asia's Health & Insurance Super App - a consumer-centric digital ecosystem that coordinates and integrates every aspect of a user’s healthcare journey from doctors consultations to health insurance management whilst bridging the online to offline divide. oDoc is the largest digital health company in Sri Lanka, serving over 200,000 users and 70+ corporates. We enable patients to get medical advice, medicines and lab tests from the comfort & safety of their own homes. We have grown revenues by 10X since 2020, and as of mid-2021, we have onboarded over 1,000 doctors across 60 specialisations. In response to COVID-19, oDoc launched the Sri Lanka National Telemedicine platform on behalf of the Ministry of Health in Sri Lanka. oDoc is Sri Lanka’s only Techstars and Stanford StartX backed company.
