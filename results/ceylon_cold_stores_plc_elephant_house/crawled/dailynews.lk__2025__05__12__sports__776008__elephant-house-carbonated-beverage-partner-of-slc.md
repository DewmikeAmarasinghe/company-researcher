# Elephant House carbonated Beverage partner of SLC
by Gayan Abeykoon May 12, 2025
May 12, 2025 1:02 am   
  
**Dhammika Ratnaweera**
Daminda Gamlath the President of Consume Foods Sector of John Keells Holdings( third from right ) briefing the media regarding official carbonated Beverage partner sponsorship .SLC CEO Ashley de Silva Dhananjaya de Silva Test captain,Charith Asalanka ODI and T -20 captain, Women’s captain Chamari Athapaththu and Jude Martin Head of Beverages Ceylon Cold Stores PLC and Vice President of John Keells Holdings also in the picture ( Picture by Sulochana Gamage) 
_142_
The Chief Executive officer of Sri Lanka Cricket Ashley de Silva said SLC partnered with Elephant House as the official carbonated Beverage partner of Sri Lanka Cricket .SLC deeply value this partnership as a commercial collaborations such as this are vital to strengthen the foundation in the cricketing journey as partners of cricket on and off the field.
The CEO further said Elephant House joined us as our teams will be engaged in number of cricket tours during next 12 months with Bangladesh arriving next June for all three tours followed by Zimbabwe tour and several other multi national tours and also hosting World cup in January – February 2026. Sri Lanka One day team climbed to ICC rank 4 th position while Women’s national team is in tremendous form defeating India and South Africa already entered the final in ongoing Tri Nation series and such performance uplift the profile of SLC and it turns at substance value of Brand to partner with us and we believe Elephant House will benefit from this and welcome Elephant House to continue long lasting partnerships with SLC said Ashley
The President Consumer Food Sector of John Keells Holdings Daminda Gamlath said we have great pleasure in joining SLC as official carbonate Beverages partner at this moment. This also brings significant milestones between our company and cricketing community in Sri Lanka . we are committed to not only supporting Sri Lanka Cricket team but also improving cricket standard of our beloved nation.
Cricket is more than just a sport in Sri Lanka and it unites all .Through this partnership we aim to develop cricket at all levels said Duminda.
Sri Lanka Test captain Dhananjaya de Silva ,ODI and T- 20 captain Charith Asalanka and women’s Captain Chamari Athapaththu also accompanied the officials at the head table at this briefing to announce Elephant House as official Carbonated Beverage partner for SLC .
