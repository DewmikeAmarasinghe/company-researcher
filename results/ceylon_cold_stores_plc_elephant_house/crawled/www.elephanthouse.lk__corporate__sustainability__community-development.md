# Community Development
Ceylon Cold Stores (CCS) is committed to the empowerment of communities across Sri Lanka with the assistance of our sibling organisations in John Keells Group. Our CSR missions reach beyond crisis relief as we strive to touch the lives across communities every day.
## Gift of Vision
CCS in partnership with John Keells Foundation (JKF) and Vision 2020 programme conducted free eye care camps for people in need of cataract treatment across Sri Lanka.
