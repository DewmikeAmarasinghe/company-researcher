# Elephant House Going Global
Driven by nearly 150 years of experience in sharing flavour and goodness with our consumers, Ceylon Cold Stores (CCS) now reaches across oceans to share the flavourful goodness of Elephant House Ice Cream and Beverages with the world.   
Represented around the world by our much beloved Elephant House Ginger Beer, and a range of carbonated soft drinks, natural fruit juices, and naturally flavoured ice creams, popsicles and lollies, we serve our consumers through an extensive network of global distributors and agents.
Our commitment to globalising unique Sri Lankan brands is supported by an extensive quality management system and vigorous research and development process, which ensures that no matter where in the world you are, you will enjoy the best of freshness and goodness in Elephant House products.
