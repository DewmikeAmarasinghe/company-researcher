# First Harvesting Programme at Elephant House “SARA MIDULA” 
"SARA MIDULA" an initiative to educate employees on home gardening, in order to promote the health & wellbeing of our communities. Through the access to and self-reliance on nutrition, reaps its first harvest. we would like to thank all elephant house employees who toiled on the project, for their hard work and resilience towards making the model farm a successful initiative. Elephant house Saara Midula is aligned with the health initiatives of the john keels foundation - the corporate social responsibility (CSR) entity of the john keels group - aimed at promoting the health and wellbeing of our communities through access to and self - reliance on nutrition.
