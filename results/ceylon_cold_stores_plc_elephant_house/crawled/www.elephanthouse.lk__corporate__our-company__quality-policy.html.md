#  Ceylon Cold Stores PLC Quality, Food Safety, Environmental and Occupational Health & Safety Policy
Ceylon Cold Stores PLC as a manufacturer, distributor, marketer and exporter of Beverages and Ice Creams, is committed to;
  * Delight customers and consumers by providing high quality beverages and ice creams which are safe, refreshing, and exciting.
  * Provide safe and healthy working conditions for all stake holders through consultation & participation of all parties and loyal to protect the environment.
  * Carryout manufacturing, procurement, marketing, distribution to our own warehouses and other activities to accomplish the above.

  
  

**Our Strategy is to :**
  * Continually improve our Quality, Food Safety, Environmental and Occupational Health & Safety Management Systems in line with the development of global standards.
  * Comply with all related national and international laws & regulations and other requirements on food quality, food safety, environmental and occupational health & safety.
  * Set and review quality, food safety, environmental and occupational health & safety objectives and targets periodically, while providing adequate information, resources and controls to ensure the accomplishment of those objectives.
  * Provide training and support to employees at all levels in view of making them aware of the company’s policy and objectives and their roles and responsibilities of delivering the same.
  * Treat our employees in line with John Keells Group value system.
  * Eliminate hazards and reduce relevant risks, identify opportunities, and maintain a high level of preparedness to manage quality, food safety, environmental, occupational health & safety incidents.
  * Work closely with government & non-governmental agencies and other stakeholders to support the nation’s pursuit of sustainable development.
