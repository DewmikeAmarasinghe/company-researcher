# Refreshment
## Refreshingly Sri Lankan
Delivering a range of refreshing flavours that tickle your taste buds, the Elephant House beverage range is a colourful treat for your senses, quenching your thirst in the best possible way. Exciting, and fun, they are made just for you, offering up the perfect amount of fizz and deliciousness, with unique pack sizes, bottle shapes and price points to suit a number of consumption moments.
An intricate part of the very culture of Sri Lanka, Elephant House is a mainstay in virtually every household, island-wide, with our distribution network spanning the length and breadth of our country, and even going beyond and conquering overseas markets.
