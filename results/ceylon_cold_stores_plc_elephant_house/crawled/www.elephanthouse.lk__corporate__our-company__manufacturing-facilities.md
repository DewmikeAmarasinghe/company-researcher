# What We Do
Ceylon Cold Stores PLC is a subsidiary of John Keells Holdings PLC, operating in the consumer food sector, which also consists of Keells Food Products PLC and Colombo Ice Company (Private) Limited.
Our beverage and ice cream manufacturing facilities include a beverage and ice cream manufacturing facility at Ranala, which handles the production of bulk range frozen confectionary and a state-of-art-ice cream manufacturing facility in Avissawella developed under the Colombo Ice Company (Private) Limited

## Distribution Network
The Ceylon Cold Stores’ extensive network of 164 distributors helps to supply our products to over 100,000 retailers island-wide ensuring the accessibility of our soft drinks, ice cream, fruit juice as well as other products to customers around the country.  
  
We continue to reap benefits from our state-of-the-art distribution system which provides access to real-time data sharing ensuring that the right product is available to the retail outlets at the right time.
