### Business description: Ceylon Cold Stores PLC
Ceylon Cold Stores PLC is a Sri Lanka-based company, which is a manufacturer of beverages and frozen confectionery. The Company sells its products under the Elephant House (EH) brand. The Company operates through two segments: Manufacturing and Supermarkets. The Manufacturing segment has developed its product offering in beverages and frozen confectionery to include a range of products that cater to diverse consumer needs and occasions. The Companyâ€™s beverage products include carbonated soda drinks, water, fruit drinks, fruit flavored tea, flavored milk, and standardized milk. The Companyâ€™s frozen confectionery products include bulk ice creams, ice cream cups, ice cream sticks, ice cream cones, ice cream bites and frozen yoghurt. It offers a range of ice creams, such as Pani Cadju Cup, JellyIce, Rockey Road, French Vanilla, Choc Choc Chips, Very Berry, Coffee, Cookie Cream, and others. The Supermarket segment focuses on modern organized retailing through a chain of supermarkets.
