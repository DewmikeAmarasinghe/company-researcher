### Ceylon Cold Stores PLC's reported carbon emissions
Ceylon Cold Stores PLC, headquartered in LK, reported a total carbon emissions of approximately 59.5 million kg CO2e for the year 2023. This total comprises 5.3 million kg CO2e from Scope 1 emissions, 43.1 million kg CO2e from Scope 2 emissions, and 9.8 million kg CO2e from Scope 3 emissions. In 2024, emissions remained consistent at approximately 59.5 million kg CO2e, with Scope 1 emissions at 2.8 million kg CO2e, Scope 2 at 56.7 million kg CO2e, and Scope 3 at 9.6 million kg CO2e. Ceylon Cold Stores has not disclosed any specific reduction targets or initiatives as part of their climate commitments. The company is a current subsidiary of a larger corporate family, with emissions data cascaded from Ceylon Cold Stores PLC itself. The absence of significant reduction targets suggests a need for enhanced climate action strategies within the organisation. Overall, Ceylon Cold Stores PLC's emissions profile highlights the importance of addressing both direct and indirect emissions to align with global climate goals.
