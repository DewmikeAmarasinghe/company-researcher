# Life at CCS
Ceylon Cold Stores PLC, with the unmatched brand name Elephant House, is an enthusiastic, exciting, fun and enjoyable place to work at. Our diverse employee engagement activities are lined up to ensure that CCS is truly “More than Just a workplace”.
### BLISSFUL NIGHT - CCS Annual Excursion 2022 
Annual Excursion of CCS Supply Chain & Support Functions was held on 22nd & 23rd July 2022. The staff members were benefited to explore the beauty of down south with a stay in Araliya Beach Resort & Spa - Unawatuna. 
The excursion was coupled with few employee engagement activities and relaxing time which enhanced the opportunity to collaborate and interact among staff members to enrich team spirit. The event was successfully concluded with a series of lovely memories to cherish.
