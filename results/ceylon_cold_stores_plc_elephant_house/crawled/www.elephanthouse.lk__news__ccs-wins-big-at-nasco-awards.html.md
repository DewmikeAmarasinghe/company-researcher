# CCS Wins Big at NASCO Awards 2019
Ceylon Cold stores PLC (CCS) achieved a significant milestone by being awarded the highest number of medals in its recent recorded history at the National Sales Congress (NASCO) organized by the Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM) held at the BMICH on October 15, 2019.  
  
NASCO is the sole credible and respected award ceremony organized annually to recognize sales professionals at a national level. Top performers of all industries are recognized and awarded for their excellent performance. This long-standing prestigious event is much sought after as companies nominate their outstanding sales professionals who are then screened by an eminent panel of judges who lay the foundation to excel in their respective careers.
