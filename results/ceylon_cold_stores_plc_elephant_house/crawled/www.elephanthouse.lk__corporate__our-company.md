# Our Company
#### Helping to live life better every day
One of the oldest and trusted food and beverage manufacturing companies in Sri Lanka, Ceylon Cold Stores (CCS) is a giant in beverage, carbonated soft drinks (CSD), and frozen confectionery market in Sri Lanka. A company that was strongly founded on the values of trust and integrity, CCS has been in operation for 153 years and is now a leading member company of John Keells Holdings Group, Sri Lanka’s largest conglomerate.
With Elephant House, the iconic beverage and ice-cream brand, CCS has touched the lives of many a Sri Lankan for generations. The entire range of Elephant House products, including ice creams, popsicles, flavoured milk products, fruit-based beverages, water, energy drinks, carbonated soft drinks and chasers, with their flavour and goodness, is an integral part of the daily lives of millions of people at home and around the world.
Our vision is to **deliver pleasure** and **nutrition** throughout **people’s lives** , **whenever** and **wherever** they choose to eat and drink.
### What We Do
CCS operates state-of-the-art factories both at Ranala and at Awissawella where we employ the current technologies while adhering to the strictest standards of quality and hygiene at every step of the production, packaging, and warehousing process. Adopting the best manufacturing practices, a highly effective and knowledgeable management team remains in charge of the manufacturing facilities to ensure that it’s continually run at optimal capacity to harness operational efficiencies and step up productivity.
