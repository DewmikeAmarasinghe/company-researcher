## DirectPay - About the company
DirectPay is a seed company based in Colombo (Sri Lanka), founded in 2017 by Kanishka Weeramunda, Dinesh Karunathilaka and Sasindu Pathiranage. It operates as a Wallet-based payments. DirectPay has raised an undisclosed amount in funding from John Keells X. The company has 6 active competitors, including 2 funded and 2 that have exited. Its top competitors include companies like MoneyGram, CarePay and People's Pay. 
### Company Details
DirectPay offers wallet-based payments to consumers. It offers services such as fund transfer, QR-based payments, bill splitting, tip payments, and many more. It facilitates account transfers between consumers and merchants. The app is available on iOS and Android devices. Website
