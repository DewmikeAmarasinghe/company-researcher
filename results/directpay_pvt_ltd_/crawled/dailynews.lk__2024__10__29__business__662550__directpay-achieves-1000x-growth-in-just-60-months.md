# DirectPay achieves 1000x growth in just 60 months
by damith October 29, 2024
October 29, 2024 1:06 am   
  

Kanishka Weeramunda, Director, Chief Executive Officer , DirectPay, Rohan Muttiah, Non-Executive Director, DirectPay, Sasindu Pathiranage, Director, Vice President, Digital Payment Platforms, DirectPay 
_29_
In just 60 months, DirectPay has accomplished an extraordinary 1000x growth, solidifying its position as a leading player in the Sri Lankan fintech space. This incredible achievement is a testament to their unwavering commitment to innovation, customer-centric solutions and their passion for transforming digital payments.
As a startup, DirectPay’s success is built on the support of key industry stakeholders. They extend their heartfelt gratitude to the Information and Communication Technology Agency (ICTA), National Best Quality ICT Awards (NBQSA), Sri Lanka Association of Software and Service Companies (SLASSCOM) and Federation of Information Technology Industry Sri Lanka (FITIS) for recognizing and celebrating their efforts through various awards and industry accolades. These milestones have motivated them to continue driving growth and enhancing the fintech landscape in Sri Lanka.
Trust in their digital payment solutions and their continuous engagements have been vital in helping them scale and innovate rapidly. From merchants to financial institutions and everyday users, their collaboration has fueled DirectPay’s growth trajectory. Their journey from a fledgling startup to achieving a 1000x growth in such a short span has been both challenging and rewarding. With this momentum, they aim to bring even more cutting-edge solutions, ensuring seamless, secure, and efficient payment services for all.
