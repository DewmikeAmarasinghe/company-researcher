## DirectPay won the Merit Award at the Asia Smart App (ASA) Awards 2020!
##### By DirectPay
Monday, 17 September 2020
We are the only Sri Lankan app to be recognized at the ceremony with a Merit Award among all the Asian apps. WTIA organized the ASA Ceremony for the 7th consecutive year, to further encourage the development of the expanding smart application industry and DirectPay is proud to have had the honour of being a part of it! 
  

Thanks to the amazing team behind DirectPay including our Co-Founder/COO, Sasindu Pathiranage for taking part in the Award Presentation Ceremony. We thank everyone who supported us in breaking the boundaries in every way with #DirectPay!
