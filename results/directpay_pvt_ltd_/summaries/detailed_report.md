## 2. Business Model and Revenue

- Core model
  - DirectPay enables merchants to receive payments digitally across the full ecosystem (mobile app, internet gateway, payment links, subscriptions, bill settlements) to improve how payments are pursued.

- Products and platforms
  - `Merchant App`, `Internet Payment Gateway`, `DirectPay Point of Sale Terminal (POS)`, `DirectPay Virtual Point of Sale (VPOS)`
  - Multichannel payment support: `Static QR`, `Dynamic QR`, `Billers`, `Government Payments`, `Merchant Wallet & Topups`, `SDKs`, `Plugins`, `APIs`
  - Payment methods and currencies: **Visa**, **Mastercard**, **American Express**, LankaQR; **LKR** and **USD**

- Revenue streams and pricing (tiered framework)
  - Processing fees (merchants)
    - **Starter**: **3.5%** per transaction; device charges; 36-month POS contract; Sunmi POS; **LKR 3,500/月**; device free with minimum monthly commitment of **LKR 500,000**.
    - **Pro**: **2.9%**; device charges; **LKR 4,000/月**; device free with minimum **LKR 650,000**.
    - **Privilege**: **2.6%**; device charges; **LKR 4,750/月**; device free with minimum **LKR 800,000**; includes Key Account Manager.
  - Cross-border and premium fees
    - Cross-border: up to **1.5%** extra; currency conversion to LKR.
    - Premium card brands: additional **1%** for AmEx/Discover/Diners Club.

- Internet Payment Gateway pricing (3 plans)
  - **Starter**: **3.5%**; no service fee; per-transaction limit **LKR 50,000**; supports links, plugins, APIs, SDKs.
  - **Pro**: **2.9%**; service fee **LKR 3,000/月**; per-transaction limit **LKR 250,000**; tokenization fee **LKR 8/card/月**; recurring payments.
  - **Privilege**: **2.6%**; service fee **LKR 8,000/月**; per-transaction limit **LKR 1,000,000**; tokenization **LKR 8/card/月**; includes Key Account Manager.

- Settlement and onboarding
  - Settlement: daily/monthly reports; real-time via `LankaClear CEFTS`; standard T+3, T+2 with Cargills Bank.
  - Onboarding: online merchant onboarding; application is free; no initial setup fee.

- Additional capabilities
  - Recurring payments, tokenization, and checkout APIs for developers; merchant and payment-link options.
