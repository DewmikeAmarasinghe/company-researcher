## 2. Business Model and Revenue
- Core model
  - Enables merchants to receive payments digitally across a full ecosystem including mobile app, internet gateway, payment links, subscriptions, and bill settlements
  - Multichannel capability supports in-person and online transactions to improve merchant cash flow
- Revenue streams
  - **Processing fees** for merchants across plans
  - Device charges and monthly commitments tied to POS contracts
  - Cross-border charges and premium card brand surcharges
  - Internet Payment Gateway pricing with tiered plans and monthly service fees
- Pricing framework (tiered)
  - **Starter**: 3.5% per transaction; device charges; 36-month POS contract; Sunmi POS; LKR 3,500/月; device free with minimum monthly commitment of LKR 500,000
  - **Pro**: 2.9% per transaction; device charges; LKR 4,000/月; minimum commitment LKR 650,000
  - **Privilege**: 2.6% per transaction; device charges; LKR 4,750/月; minimum commitment LKR 800,000; includes Key Account Manager
- Internet Gateway plans
  - **Starter**: 3.5%; no service fee; per-transaction limit LKR 50,000
  - **Pro**: 2.9%; LKR 3,000/月; limit LKR 250,000; tokenization LKR 8/card/月; recurring payments
  - **Privilege**: 2.6%; LKR 8,000/月; limit LKR 1,000,000; tokenization LKR 8/card/月; includes Key Account Manager
- Settlement and onboarding
  - Settlement via daily/monthly reports; real-time via LankaClear CEFTS; standard T+3 or T+2 with Cargills Bank
  - Online merchant onboarding; application is free with no initial setup fee
- Additional capabilities
  - Recurring payments tokenization and checkout APIs for developers; merchant wallets; payment links; cross-border considerations

*Insights*: Business model centers on tiered merchant pricing with a mix of recurring and per-transaction charges; cross-border fees and premium card surcharges add margin flexibility
