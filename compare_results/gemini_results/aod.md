This lead enrichment report provides a comprehensive overview of **AOD South Asia (Pvt) Ltd** (Academy of Design), a premier design education and innovation hub based in Sri Lanka.

### 1. Founder & Leadership Team

* **Founder & Chairperson:** **Linda Speldewinde**, a renowned entrepreneur and national icon in Sri Lanka's creative sector.
* **Current Leadership / Management:**
* **Dean of Academics:** Derek Black
* **Director of Academic Affairs:** Professor Thomass Atkinson
* **Key Faculty Leaders:** Kat Scott (Fashion Design), Sara Snowdon (Fashion Marketing), Chani Perera (Visual Communication), and Yamini Balasubramaniam (Interior Design).
* **Creative Director/Consultant:** Natharlea Yahampath.



### 2. Business Model & Strategy

* **Business Model:** An "Education-to-Enterprise" model. It integrates academic degree programs with industry-facing platforms (Festivals, E-commerce, Innovation Hubs).
* **Revenue Streams:** Tuition fees from undergraduate and postgraduate programs, corporate training, design consultancy, and event-based revenue (SLDF).
* **Pricing Strategy:** Positioned as a premium educational provider. By partnering with Northumbria University (UK), they offer British degrees at approximately **1/3 of the cost** of studying in the UK, making global education accessible locally.

### 3. Products & Services Offerings

* **Undergraduate Degrees (Northumbria University, UK):** Fashion & Textile Design, Fashion Marketing, Interior Design, Visual Communication Design, and Motion Graphics & Animation.
* **Postgraduate Degrees (De Montfort University, UK):** MA Design Innovation, MA Design Management & Entrepreneurship, and MA Contour Fashion.
* **Foundation Programs:** Design Foundation for post-O/L students.
* **Design Initiatives:** Sri Lanka Design Festival (SLDF), Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week (MBFW) Sri Lanka, and Urban Island (sustainable craft brand).

### 4. History & Growth Timeline

* **Founded:** Early 2000s (approx. 20 years ago) by Linda Speldewinde.
* **2010:** Launched the first Sri Lanka Design Festival (SLDF).
* **Expansion:** Partnered with Northumbria University to offer 100% internal British degrees.
* **2021+:** Established the **Colombo Innovation Tower (CIT)** as a central hub for South Asian design and innovation.
* **2025-2026:** Expanding into "City-wide" design initiatives like "What's Your Colombo?" to position the city as a creative capital.

### 5. Competitors & Market Position

* **Direct Competitors:** AMDT (Academy of Multimedia Design & Technology), Raffles Design Institute (Sri Lanka), and local universities offering design tracks (e.g., University of Moratuwa).
* **Industry Position:** Market leader in private design education in Sri Lanka, distinguished by its strong UK university affiliations and industry-integrated festival platforms.
