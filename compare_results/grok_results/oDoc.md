## 2. What is the business model, revenue streams, pricing strategy of oDoc Sri Lanka?

- **Business Model**: Digital health platform for telemedicine. Primarily B2B2C, partnering with corporates, insurers, and telecoms to provide subscriptions. Also B2C via app. Focus on accessibility via mobile tech (app, SMS, video). 

- **Revenue Streams**:
  - Corporate subscriptions (monthly per-employee fees).
  - Per-consultation fees (B2C).
  - Partnerships (e.g., telecom billing, insurance integrations).
  - Medicine delivery and lab services fees.

- **Pricing Strategy**: Affordable, subscription-based for unlimited access (e.g., Rs. 99-499/month). Per-consult ~Rs. 500-1000. Flexible during crises (free COVID consults). Targets low-income via telecom partnerships.
